Obligations for transfers; a) Calculation of resultsIncome and expenditure, as well as income and expenses, are recorded monthly in accordance with the accrual regime, which establishes that income and expenditure, revenue and expenses must be included in the calculation of the results of the periods in which they occur, always simultaneously when they are correlated, regardless of receipt or payment, allocated in a manner proportional according to the amounts of the gross input of the cooperative act and the gross revenue from non-cooperative acts, when not identified with each activity.
