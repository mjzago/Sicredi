Income and expenditure, as well as income and expenses, are recorded monthly in accordance with the accrual basis, which establishes that income and expenses expenditures and revenues and expenses must be included in the calculation of the results of the periods in which they occur, always simultaneously when they are correlated, regardless of receipt or payment, allocated in a proportional manner according to the amounts of the gross inflow of cooperative act and of gross revenue from non-cooperative acts, when not identified with each activity.
