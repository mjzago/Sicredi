CMN Resolution nº 4,748, establishes that financial institutions and other institutions authorized to operate by the Central Bank of Brazil must observe the Technical Pronouncement CPC 46 – Fair Value Measurement (CPC46) in the measurement of equity and income elements, in situations in which the measurement at the fair value of such elements are provided for in specific regulations.Update (“accrual”) of credit operations due within 59 days is accounted for in income from credit operations, and from the 60th day onwards, in income to be appropriated.
