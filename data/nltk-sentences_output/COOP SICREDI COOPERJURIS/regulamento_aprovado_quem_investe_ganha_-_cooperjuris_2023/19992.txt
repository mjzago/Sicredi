Once in possession of the participation coupon, the member must fill it out with their legible identification data, namely: name and CPF or Page of 2 6CNPJ, as well as correctly answer the promotional question, for later deposit in the ballot box located at the branch where the participant carried out the financial transaction and has its respective account, as described in this regulation (ANNEX I) Coupon(s) filled in with a stamp will be accepted as long as they contain the mandatory data mentioned above.
