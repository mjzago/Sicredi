Non-banking financial institution, authorized to operate by the Central Bank of Brazil, which began its activities on 08/13/2006 and its main objectives are: i) Developing savings programs, appropriate use of credit and provision of services, carrying out all its own active, passive and ancillary operations of credit unions; ii) Provide, through mutuality, financial assistance to members in their specific activities; iii) Work on training education of its members, in order to encourage cooperativism.
