Following the succession plan, in which the executive mandates 2018- 2020, the president of Central Sicredi South/Southeast Fernando Dall'Agnese was elected in the 2021 assemblies as the new President of the Board of Directors of SicrediPar, the Confederation Sicredi, the Banco Cooperativo Sicredi, the Board of Sicredi Fundos Garantidores and the Foundation's Board of Trustees.
