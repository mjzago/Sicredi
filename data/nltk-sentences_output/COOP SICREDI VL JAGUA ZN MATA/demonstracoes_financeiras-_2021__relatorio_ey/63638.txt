g) Provision for expected losses associated with credit risk The provision for expected losses associated with credit risk is based on the analysis of operations and takes into account the economic situation, past experience, the specific and global risks of the portfolios, considering the provisioning criteria, defined in Resolutions nº 2,682/99 and nº 2,697/00 of the CMN, associated with the assessments carried out by the Administration, in determining the risks of credit.The portfolio is made up of fixed income securities, variable income securities and investment funds, which are presented at cost plus income earned to date balance sheet, adjusted to the respective market values, as applicable.
