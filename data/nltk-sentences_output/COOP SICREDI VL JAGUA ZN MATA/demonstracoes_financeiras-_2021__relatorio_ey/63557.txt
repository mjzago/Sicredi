VI - Liquidity Risk The processes for managing the socio-environmental risk of the Sicredi system include: • Internal standards containing rules, methodologies and responsibilities regarding the management of theme; • Monitoring delimitations and fences of exposures subject to socio-environmental risk, in line with the system's risk appetite; • Collection and use of data to measure, classify and evaluate social, environmental and climate risks in operations; • Periodic carrying out stress tests for socio-environmental risk scenarios; • Interlocution and reporting to environmental bodies, federations, business partners and governance forums; •       Evolution constant of the strategy on the topic, aiming at alignment with market techniques and technologies, as well as the expectations of interested parties.
