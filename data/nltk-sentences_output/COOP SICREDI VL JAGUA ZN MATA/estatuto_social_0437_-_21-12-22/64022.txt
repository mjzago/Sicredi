The surplus determined at the end of each year (consolidated results) will be allocated as follows: I – 70% (seventy percent), at least, for the reserve fund, intended to repair losses and support the development of the Cooperative; II – 7% (seven percent), at least, to the Technical, Educational and Social Assistance Fund (FATES), intended to provide assistance to members, their families, Cooperative employees and the community located in their area of action; III - the remaining balance will be made available to the general assembly, for purposes it deems appropriate, complied with the provisions of § 1 of this article.
