The Board of Directors is governed by the following rules: I - meets, on an ordinary monthly basis, and extraordinarily whenever necessary, upon call by its President, the majority of the Board itself; II - deliberates, validly, by a simple majority of votes, the majority of its components were present, with the President having the tie-breaking vote; III - the Board's deliberations and other substantial occurrences at the meetings will be recorded in minutes, approved and signed by the members present.
