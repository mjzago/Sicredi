Compliance risk management, within the scope of Sicredi, is under the responsibility of the Compliance Superintendence, an integral structure of Banco Cooperativo Sicredi S.A, which, for monitoring the compliance risk management processes include: • Identification of the institution's compliance risks ; • Communication, qualification and training of all levels of the Entity to properly manage compliance risks and complying with legal and (self)regulatory requirements; • Monitoring and monitoring of relevant processes, actions taken to mitigate compliance risks and correct deficiencies, with the aim of promoting compliance.
