The Fund offers assistance on three fronts: • Technical assistance: supports technical training activities to develop the economic activity of members, improving the conditions of professional activity and supporting family subsistence; • Educational assistance: aims to improve the student’s knowledge membership and collaborators of the cooperative with the development of educational activities to disseminate and encourage cooperativism; • Social assistance: aims to improve the lives of associates, their families and employees through social support, with events, sporting and cultural activities, among other initiatives.
