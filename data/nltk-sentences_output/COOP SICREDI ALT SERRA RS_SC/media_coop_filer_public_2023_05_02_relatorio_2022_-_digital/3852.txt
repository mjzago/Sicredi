The Altos da Serra RS/SC cooperative currently has 42 Accredited Agents who form a large network where members or non-members can pay water, electricity, telephone bills, billing slips, taxes, make deposits and other banking services with all facility.The partner network also extends to more than 200 partner companies (in the resale and sales of vehicles, construction and renovation, machinery and equipment, solar energy and CDC - furniture and appliances, electronics, aesthetics and health).
