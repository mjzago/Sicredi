Among the main risks managed by institution, the Capital Management Structure, Operational Risk, Market, Liquidity, and Credit stand out, the structures of which are presented below: I - Capital Management Structure For the purposes of current legislation, Capital Management is defined as the continuous process of: • Monitoring and control of the capital maintained by the Institution; • Assessment of the need for capital to face the risks to which the Institution is subject; • Goal planning and of capital needs, considering the Institution's strategic objectives.
