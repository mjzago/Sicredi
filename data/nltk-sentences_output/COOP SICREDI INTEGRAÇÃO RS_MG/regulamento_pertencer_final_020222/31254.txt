The following will be considered to be elected nucleus coordinators: I - the member with the most votes and the second most voted by the nucleus will be respectively considered effective coordinator and substitute coordinator, and so on depending on the number of substitutes; II - the members with the highest number of votes, when presented as candidates jointly (effective and substitute(s)), indicating the order of substitutes; III - the members presented as the only candidates (effective and substitute(s)), acclaimed by the Core Assembly.
