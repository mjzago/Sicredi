EXTRAORDINARY PRE-ASSEMBLIES: As provided in § 2 and § 3 of Article 25 of the Bylaws of SICREDI CEARÁ CENTRO NORTE - Credit Cooperative of the Central North Region of Ceará, 13 (thirteen) Extraordinary Pre-General Assemblies were held (Pre -AGE), in each of the Cooperative's Service Stations (PA), called according to notices published in the newspaper “Diário do Nordeste”, pages 09, 10 and 11, Classifieds section, edition of 02/12/2020.
