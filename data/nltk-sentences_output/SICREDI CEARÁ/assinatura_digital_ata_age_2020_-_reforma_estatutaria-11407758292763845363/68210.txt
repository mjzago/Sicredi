The President of the Board of Directors of SICREDI CEARA CENTRO NORTE - Credit Cooperative of the Central North Region of Ceará, using the powers conferred on him by Article 26 of the Bylaws and Article 38 of the Delegate Election Regulations, convenes the delegates able to vote, which on this date total 67 (sixty-seven) delegates, to meet at an EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL ASSEMBLY, to be held on March 28, 2020 (Saturday), in the hall Sonata I of the Hotel Sonata de Iracema, located at Avenida Beira Mar nº 848 - Praia de Iracema, CEP 60.165 -120, Fortaleza – CE, in a different location from the headquarters of the Cooperative due to there not being enough physical space to accommodate the participants; on first call, at 1:00 pm with the presence of 2/3 (two thirds) of the delegates from each Service Station; in a second call at 2:00 pm, with the presence of half plus one of the delegates from each Service Station, and, in a third and last call at 3:00 pm, with the presence of at least 10 (ten) delegates, to deliberate on the following: AGENDA OF THE DAY.
