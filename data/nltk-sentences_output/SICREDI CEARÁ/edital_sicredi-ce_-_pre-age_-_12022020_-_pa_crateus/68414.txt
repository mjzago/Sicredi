Observations: a) As determined by Article 35 of the Cooperative's Bylaws, this Pre-General Assembly will be restricted exclusively to the aforementioned agenda, in consideration of the legal and doctrinal principles of the binding of the convening act and the unalterability of the notice of convocation; b) In the sub-items of item “1” of the agenda subject to voting, all votes will be counted, firstly those “in favor”, then the “against” and finally the “abstentions”, determined separately for each of the Service Stations, requiring the votes of 2/3 (two thirds) of the members present at the time of each vote, to make the votes valid.
