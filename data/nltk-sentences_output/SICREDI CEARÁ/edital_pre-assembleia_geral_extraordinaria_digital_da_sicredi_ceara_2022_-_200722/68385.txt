Observations: a) As determined by Article 35 of the Cooperative's Bylaws, this Pre-General Assembly will be restricted exclusively to the aforementioned agenda, in compliance with the legal and doctrinal principles of binding the convening act and the inalterability of the notice of convocation; b) In deliberative votes, only valid votes will be considered, that is, “for” and “against”; c) The deliberations taken at the Pre-Joint General Assembly, bind their respective delegates to maintain the same decision at the General Assembly of Delegates, as provided in Article 40 of the Rules for the Election of Delegates; d) If the effective Delegate and the alternate are absent at the Pre-Assembly, both will be prevented from voting at the General Assembly of Delegates, as provided in Article 40 § 4 of the Rules for the Election of Delegates; e) The member must have updated their telephone number and mailing address e-mail at the cooperative, through the Sicredi application (APP), Internet Banking or by going to your branch; f) Instructions for participation and voting in the Digital Assembly Tool: Members must access the tool through the website www.sicredi.com.br/assembleiadigital using any device with internet access.
