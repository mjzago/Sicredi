The Assembly will be held digitally for all members, using the Digital Assembly Tool accessed through the website www.sicredi.com.br/assembleiadigital, to deliberate on the following matters, which make up the agenda: 1) Election of Members of the Fiscal Council for the 2022/2024 biennium, being: a) Voting time: from the installation of the electoral process at the beginning of the assembly, until at 7:30 pm; b) Modality: Digital, using the Digital Assembly Tool, with the member being able to vote using their cell phone, tablet, private computer or electronic equipment available at the branches.
