The surplus determined at the end of each year (consolidated result) will be allocated as follows: I - 60% (sixty percent), at least, to the reserve fund, intended to repair losses and meet development of the Cooperative; II 05% (five percent), at least, for the Fund Technical, Educational and Social Assistance (FATES), aimed at providing assistance to members, their families, Cooperative employees and the community located in its area of ​​action; III 01% (one percent), for the Social Fund, intended to meet social actions of collective interest, contributing to the social development of communities in the Cooperative's area of ​​action; IV - the remaining balance will be made available to the general assembly, for purposes it deems appropriate convenient, in compliance with the provisions of § 1 of this article.
