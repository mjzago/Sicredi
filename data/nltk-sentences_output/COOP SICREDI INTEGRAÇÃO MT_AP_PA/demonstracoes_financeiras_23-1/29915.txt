The main accounting practices adopted for the preparation of the financial statements were: a) Calculation of the result Income and expenditure, as well as revenue and expenses, are recorded monthly in accordance with the jurisdiction regime, which establishes that inputs and expenditures and revenues and expenses must be included in the calculation of the results of the periods in which they occur, always simultaneously when they are correlated, regardless of receipt or payment, allocated in a proportional manner according to the amounts of the gross income from the cooperative act and the gross income from non-cooperative act, when not identified with each activity.
