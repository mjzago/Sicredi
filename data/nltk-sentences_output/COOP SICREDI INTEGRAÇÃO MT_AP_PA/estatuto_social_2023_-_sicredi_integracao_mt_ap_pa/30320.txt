§ 12º The Share Capital may be refunded to the terminated member, before the general meeting referred to in § 10º 9º of this article takes place, provided that: I - the partial result of the year in which the dismissal occurs presents surpluses; II - there are no losses to be compensated with future leftovers; and III - the financial and asset conditions of the Cooperative are considered, respecting its compliance with all asset limits required by current legislation.
