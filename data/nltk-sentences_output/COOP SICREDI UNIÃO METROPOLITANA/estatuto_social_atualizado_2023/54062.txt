The surpluses determined at the end of each year (consolidated results) will be allocated as follows: I - 65% (sixty-five percent), at least, to the reserve fund, intended to repair losses and to attend to the development of the Cooperative; II - 5% (five percent), at least, for the Technical, Educational and Social (FATES), aimed at providing assistance to members, their families, Cooperative employees and the community located in its area of ​​action; III – 12% (twelve percent), at least, for the Development and Expansion Fund, intended to support operational expenses for opening, renovating or maintaining service stations and promoting promotion, expansion and development actions of the Cooperative , in accordance with its own Regulations; IV – 3% (three percent), at least, for the Social Development Fund, intended to meet social actions of collective interest, contributing to the social development of communities in the Cooperative's area of ​​action; V - the remaining balance will be made available to the general assembly, for purposes it deems appropriate, in compliance with the provisions of § 1 of this article.
