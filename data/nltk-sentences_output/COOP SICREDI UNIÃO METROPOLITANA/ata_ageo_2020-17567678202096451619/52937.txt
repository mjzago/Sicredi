The main changes consist of improving the rules that deal with the Electoral Commission, regarding requirements for its composition; possibility for the Electoral Commission to apply penalties to the Candidate(s) and decide on any challenges to their registration 10 days before the first Core Assembly; You may also, for a single time, determine the replacement of candidate(s) who do not meet the corresponding requirements; new rule regarding the possibility of recomposing the approved plate(s) after the publication of the final nomination.
