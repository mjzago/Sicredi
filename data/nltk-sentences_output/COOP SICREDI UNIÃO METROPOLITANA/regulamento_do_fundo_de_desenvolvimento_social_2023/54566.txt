After carrying out/completing the project, the entity must insert the financial statement on the Social Development Fund website where the project registration was made, consisting of: • Report containing the project description executed and its benefits; • Photos of the finished project; • Invoices or receipts that prove the use of the full amount (invoices must be in the name and CNPJ of the entity covered and issued after receipt of the resources, during the project execution period, and must correspond to the budgeted items.
