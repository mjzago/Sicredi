At the Cooperativa Sicredi Fronteiras PR/SC/SP, a pioneer in the state of Paraná with the A U nião Faz a Vida Program, in 2022 there were more than 260 projects developed by the networks of teaching in the municipalities of Capanema (PR), Santo Antônio (PR), Capitão Leônidas Marques (PR) and Itupeva (SP), and in all of them, three pedagogical consultancies were carried out during the year, and several ongoing training sessions, providing moments training with teachers.
