In addition to other duties arising from legislation and these Bylaws, it is the responsibility of the Board of Directors, observing the details provided for in systemic regulations: I - establish the strategic direction of the Cooperative and monitor the execution and fulfillment of the planning approved by it; II - monitor and supervise the performance of the Cooperative's Executive Board in view of the objectives and goals defined for the Society; III - approve regulations within its competence, which cannot contradict the provisions of systemic regulations; IV - appoint and dismiss the members of the Executive Board, as well as establish their duties and competencies and the individual remuneration of Directors, in compliance with the provisions contained in the bylaws; V - register the vacancy of members of the Board of Directors and the Executive Board; VI - authorize the contracting of credit operations with financial institutions, intended to finance the activities of members, in compliance with the provisions of systemic regulations; VII - decide on the payment of annual remuneration on shares - capital shares, stipulating the remuneration, in accordance with current legislation; Approved at the Extraordinary General Meeting of 04/17/2023 VIII - forward to the general meeting a proposal for the donation of the Cooperative's real estate, classified as non-current; IX - decide on the acquisition, sale or encumbrance of the Cooperative's real estate, classified as non-current; X - examine and investigate reports of infractions committed within the scope of Society, including those sent to them by the Executive Board and the Fiscal Council, and determine the application of applicable penalties; XI - decide on the elimination and exclusion of members; XII - decide on the call for a general meeting; XIII - authorize, in advance, capital participation in other companies or entities, including those of a non-cooperative nature, meeting complementary or accessory purposes, in the interests of the membership and the Cooperative community and respecting current legislation and systemic deliberations and guidelines in this regard; XIV - authorize the alteration of the address of the headquarters, within the same municipality, as well as to deliberate on the opening, closure, transfer or change of address of the other dependencies or branches of the cooperative, within or outside the headquarters, in the terms current legislation; XV - comply with and enforce this Statute, legislation and internal regulations from Sicredi; XVI - express an opinion on the management report and financial statements for the year; XVII - choose and dismiss external auditors, in compliance with systemic guidelines; XVIII - approve policies for attracting new members and/or increasing social capital among members; XIX - authorize the carrying out of campaigns and the offering or distribution of bonuses, prizes or other advantages aimed at attracting members or increasing the social capital by membership; Approved at the Extraordinary General Meeting of 04/17/2023 case.
