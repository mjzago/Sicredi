In a continuous act, the President started the Extraordinary General Assembly, and passed the Agenda: Item 1 - Proposal for a broad reform of the Bylaws of Sicredi Coomamp: Aiming at partial adaptation to Sicredi's Systemic Standard Statute, the text containing the current wording, the proposed wording, the deleted items and the renumberings of the articles as necessary was presented to the plenary session, the President began reading the changes, and the amendments were put to a vote at large statutory reform, was approved with 18 votes in favor, 2 against and 2 abstentions from the members who voted on the platform.
