Independent auditor's report on the financial statements To the Administrators, Advisors and Cooperative Members of Sicredi Coomamp - Coop de Crédito dos Memb de Insti Pub das Carreiras Jurid e Serv Pub Fed Estad e Munic em São Luís/MA and Munic Circunv São Luís - MA Opinion We examined the financial statements of Sicredi Coomamp - Coop de Crédito dos Memb de Insti Pub das Carreiras Jurid and Serv Pub Fed Estad e Munic in São Luís/MA and Munic Circunv (“Cooperative”) which comprise the balance sheet as of December 31, 2022 and the respective statements of surpluses or losses, comprehensive income, changes in net equity and cash flows for the year ending on that date, as well as the corresponding explanatory notes, including a summary of the main accounting policies.
