The following will be considered as elected core coordinators: I - the most voted and the second most voted by the nucleus will respectively be considered effective coordinator and substitute coordinator, and so on depending on the greater number of substitutes; II - the members with the highest number of votes, when presented as candidates jointly (effective and alternate(s)), the order of substitute(s) indicated; III - the members presented as the only candidates (effective and substitute(s)), acclaimed by the Core Assembly.
