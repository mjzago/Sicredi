To apply and carry out the activities of center coordinator, the applicant must: I - have certification in the Crescer Program; II - make use of at least 4 (four) cooperative financial solutions; III - not hold a party political position or function upon election, or during the exercise of the mandate, in compliance with the provisions of the Social Statute of the company operative; IV - not respond, due to pending issues related to protest of titles, legal charges, issuance of bad checks, non-compliance with obligations and other similar occurrences or circumstances, in addition to not having a negative record in any databases, external or within the cooperative itself , as well as having promoted or being promoting, as a party or attorney-in-fact, legal action against the cooperative itself or any other entity that is part of Sicredi; V - not to be employee of the cooperative or having been dismissed from the cooperative for just cause; and VI - not be a former counselor or ex-director who has been subject to an internal investigation to investigate violations of legal or systemic norms during the course of his term of office or who, for the same reasons, has been removed or resigned from the position for which he was held elected.
