The events addressed topics such as measuring our positive impact at a systemic and regional, planning and challenges for 2023, the highlights of 2022, the launch of Sicredi's ESG Guide, as well as lectures on current topics such as: • Climate change in the financial market; • The importance of sustainable finance in business development and generating positive impact; • Cooperativism as an inducer of local development and financial inclusion; • The Sustainability Committee as an important governance mechanism and driver in the evolution of the sustainability performance; • The importance of sustainability for building a more prosperous society and how to build a legacy.
