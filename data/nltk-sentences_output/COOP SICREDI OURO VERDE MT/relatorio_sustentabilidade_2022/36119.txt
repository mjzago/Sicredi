Responsibilities of Sicredi's management Sicredi's management is responsible for: • Selecting and establishing appropriate criteria for preparing the information contained in the Report; • Prepare indicators in accordance with the criteria and guidelines of the Global Reporting Initiative (“GRI Standards”), of the Sustainability Accounting Standards Board – Banco Comercial (“SASB”) and its own criteria regarding sustainability metrics; • Design, implement and maintain internal control over information relevant to the preparation of the sustainability indicators and metrics contained in the Report, which are free from material distortion, regardless of whether caused by fraud or error.
