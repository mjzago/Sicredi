Below we list the main social businesses carried out in 2021: partnership with Sescoop to offer Administration and Fiscal Council courses; encouraging the professional training of members and their dependents through partnerships with Senac, Sebrae and Senai; distribution of "Turma da Mônica" comic books on financial education; participation in the “More Solidarity Christmas” campaign, giving gifts to the elderly, children and needy people in the community; and sponsorship of events promoted by cooperative members, both scientific and social and charitable.
