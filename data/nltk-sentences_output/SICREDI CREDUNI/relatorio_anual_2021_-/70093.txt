In 2021, the following requests were met: Donation of 500 N95 masks and more than 2,000 surgical masks to the Pedro I Municipal Hospital; Donation of 2 air conditioning units to the Elpídio de Almeida Health Institute (ISEA Maternity) for the covid-19 ward; Donation of 3 wheelchairs and more than 600 N95 masks to the Hospital das Clínicas de Campina Grande; Donation of 40 basic food baskets to the Pastoral da Alimentação (Movement of Food Aid) from the Diocesan Seminary Parish; Donation of 60 basic food baskets to the Social Campaign of the Disciples of Jesus Group.
