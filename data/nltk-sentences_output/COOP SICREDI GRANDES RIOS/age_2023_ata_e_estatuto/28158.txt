The Board of Directors is governed by the following rules: I - meets, ordinarily, at least every 2 (two) months, and extraordinarily whenever necessary, upon call by its President, the majority of the Board itself , or even at the request of the Fiscal Council; II - deliberates, validly, by a simple majority of votes, with the majority of its components present, with the President having the tie-breaking vote; III - the deliberations of the Board and other substantial events at the meetings will be recorded in minutes, approved and signed by the members present, the contents of which the President of the Board must also inform the Cooperative's Supervisory Board promptly.
