The surpluses determined at the end of each year (consolidated result) will be allocated as follows: I - 45% (forty-five percent), at least, to the reserve fund, intended to repair losses and attend to the development of Cooperative; II - 05% (five percent), at least, to the Technical, Educational and Social Assistance Fund (FATES), intended to provide assistance to members, their families, Cooperative employees and the community located in its area of action; III - 05% (five percent), in accordance with the decision of the Board of Directors, for the Expansion Fund intended to promote promotion, expansion and development actions of the Cooperative, in accordance with its own Regulations; IV - 01% (one percent), in accordance with the decision of the Board of Directors, for the Social Fund, intended to encourage social actions of collective interest, contributing to the social development of communities in the Cooperative's area of ​​action, under the terms the specific Regulation; V - recomposition of resources from the guarantee funds, in accordance with current legislation; VI - the remaining balance will be made available to the general assembly, for purposes it deems appropriate, complied with the provisions of § 1 of this article.
