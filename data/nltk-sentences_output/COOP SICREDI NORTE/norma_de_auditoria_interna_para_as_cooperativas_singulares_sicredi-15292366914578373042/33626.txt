The Internal Audit assessment must consider full compliance with the specific regulations of the regulatory body and, among other aspects, the ability of the Singular Cooperative to: • Identify and adequately manage its risks; • Comply with official and internal standards; • Promote ethical conduct in defining its objectives, programs, activities and businesses; • Support Sicredi's strategies and objectives through adequate governance of processes, including the Information Technology process; • Seek quality and continuous improvement of its processes and controls.
