To participate in the digital assembly, the member must follow the following steps: a) Check that your telephone number and email address are updated on Sicredi (APP), Internet Banking or go to your cooperative; b) Through the website sicredi.com.br/assembleia s/, register by creating a password; c) On the day and time of the Assembly, access the website sicredi.com.br/assembleia s/ again, enter your CPF and registered password, click on your cooperative's event and you will be able to participate in discussions and votes on the items in the Assembly.
