To participate in the digital assembly, the member must follow the following steps: a) Check that the your phone number and email address are updated on Sicredi (APP), Internet Banking or go to your cooperative; b) Through the website www.sicredi.com.br/assembleiadigital, register by creating a password; c) On the day and time of the Assembly, access the website www.sicredi.com.br/assembleiadigital again, enter your CPF and registered password, click on your cooperative's event and you will be able to participate in the discussions and votes on the items in the Assembly.
