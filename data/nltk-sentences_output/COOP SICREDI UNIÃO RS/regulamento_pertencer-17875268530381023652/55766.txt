The following will be considered to be elected nucleus coordinators: I - the member with the most votes and the second most voted by the nucleus will respectively be considered effective coordinator and substitute coordinator, and so on depending on whether there is a greater number of substitutes; II - the members with the highest number of votes, when presented as candidates jointly (effective and substitute(s)), the order of substitute(s) being indicated; III - the members presented as the only candidates (effective and substitute(s)), acclaimed by the Core Assembly.
