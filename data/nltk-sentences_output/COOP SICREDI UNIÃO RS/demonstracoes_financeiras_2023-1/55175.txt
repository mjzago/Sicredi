The main accounting practices adopted for the preparation of the financial statements were: a) Calculation of the result Income and expenses, as well as revenues and expenses, are recorded monthly in accordance with the competence regime, which establishes that income and expenses must be included in the calculation of the results of the periods in which they occur, always simultaneously when they are correlated, regardless of receipt or payment, allocated proportionally according to the amounts of gross income from a cooperative act and gross revenue from a non-cooperative act, when not identified with each activity.
