TOUR: DESTINATIONS OF COOPERATIVISM LECTURES IN THE COMMUNITY Counselors and former counselors who participated in the union of cooperatives (Serro Azul, Missões and Grande Santa Rosa) in 2010, which formed the current Sicredi União, participated in a commemorative moment, on October 18, at Administrative Headquarters of Sicredi União RS/ES, in Santa Rosa/RS, and were able to verify the growth of the Cooperative and the development promoted in the communities since their participation.
