MEETING OF COUNCILORS AND FORMER COUNSELORS 76 ANNUAL REPORT 2023 - COMMEMORATIVE Held on 08 November, the event sought to celebrate, in addition to the Cooperative's anniversary, the two years of operation of Sicredi União RS/ES in Espírito Santo, with local authorities and businesspeople, presenting the work of the Sicredi and its contribution to the development of the South region.
