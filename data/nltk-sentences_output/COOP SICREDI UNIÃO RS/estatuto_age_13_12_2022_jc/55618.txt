Among other duties arising from legislation, of this Statute and Sicredi's internal regulations, the Fiscal Council is responsible for: I - exercising regular supervision over the assets, operations with associates, services and the actions of administrators; II - assiduously control financial movements, availability of resources, expenses, investments and the regularity of their implementation, as well as the values ​​and documents under custody; III - evaluate the loan policy and monitor its concession; IV - analyze the financial statements prepared periodically by the Cooperative and give an opinion on the regularity of the management accounts and the financial statements for the fiscal year, preparing the respective opinion, which will contain, if applicable, the dissenting votes, as well as compliance with the standards on the social activities and interests of the Cooperative, presenting an opinion to the general assembly, being able to consult internal and external auditors to provide information necessary to perform its functions; V - become aware of the internal audit reports produced by the Central's auditors and by the independent audit, contributing to the work of these professionals and firmly demanding, from the administration, the corrections whose need is indicated in the documents; VI - investigate compliance, by the Cooperative's administration, with the provisions of this Statute and other official regulations and the System itself, as well as as well as the deliberations of the general assembly, the Board of Directors and other systemic deliberative bodies, formally established, in relation to strategic-corporate matters of interest to all the individual cooperatives and their central members of Sicredi; VII - report to the board of directors the conclusions of their work, alerting them to irregularities found and, in the absence of action on their part, reporting the fact, in due course, to the general assembly and the Central;
