The provision corresponds to the current rates for IRPJ, which is 15%, plus an additional 10% and CSLL, in which the rate went from 15% to 20% from July 2021 and will return to 15% from January 2022, applied to the profit earned, after the calculation base adjustments provided for in the legislation and the compensation of tax losses and negative calculation base CSLL from previous years, this deduction being limited to 30% of taxable profit.
