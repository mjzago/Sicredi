The centralized structure is responsible for establishing the processes, policies and systems that support System entities in managing risk of liquidity.The processes and policies for managing liquidity risk are established following the criteria of current regulations, aligned with best market practices, and approved by the competent authorities of each System Institution.The processes for managing liquidity risk of the Sicredi System include: To measure and control this risk in Sicredi, the economic value (EVE) and financial intermediation results (NII) approaches are used.
