The Sicredi System's capital management processes include: • Capital targets at levels above the minimum legal requirements and that reflect the system's risk appetite, aiming to maintain capital to support the risks incurred and ensure business growth in a sustainable manner and efficient; • Periodic management reports on capital adequacy for the board of directors and the board of directors; • Periodic stress tests for operational risk scenarios; Furthermore, a set of procedures has been implemented to assess, manage and monitor the operational risk arising from outsourced services relevant to the regular functioning of the institution.
