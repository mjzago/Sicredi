Processes and actions aimed at information security aim to maintain of risks at acceptable levels, including the use of adequate and effective controls, in view of costs, technology and business objectives.Banco Cooperativo Sicredi is responsible for the set of policies, strategies and methodologies aimed at controlling and managing exposures to the credit risk of the companies that make up the System, having as its main responsibilities : respond to corporate credit risk management policies; develop and propose risk classification methodologies credit, including through quantitative models; assess and control capital requirements to cover assumed credit risk; and carry out constant monitoring of exposures subject to credit risk of all Sicredi companies.
