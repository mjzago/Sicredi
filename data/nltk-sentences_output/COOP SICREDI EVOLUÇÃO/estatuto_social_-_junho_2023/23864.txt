The Cooperative will have a Board of Directors, a collegial deliberation body, composed of individual members, with 13 (thirteen) advisors, among them, one President and a Vice-President, constituting basic conditions for the candidacy and exercise of the position, in accordance with the requirements established below and without prejudice to the fulfillment of other complementary requirements set out in Sicredi's internal regulations: I - not maintain, since the candidacy, employment relationship with any entity that is part of Sicredi, or even with a member of the administrative, fiscal or Executive Board of the Cooperative; II - not be a spouse or partner, nor is he related up to the 2nd (second) degree, in a direct or collateral line, with members of the Administration and Fiscal Councils and the Executive Board; III - not be, simultaneously, an employee or administrator or hold a stake in a company that, due to its activities, is considered to be a competitor of any of the entities that make up Sicredi or in whose capital they participate; IV - not have held or be holding a political position or function supporters, in the last civil year; V - meet the professional qualifications required for the position, in accordance with current legislation and in accordance with systemic regulations, compatible with the complexity of the inherent activities; VI - not having taken advantage of 2 (two) or more debt renegotiations at the Cooperative or at another Sicredi entity, in the last 2 (two) financial years; VII - not respond, nor any company of which it is controlling or administrator, due to pending issues relating to protest of securities, legal charges, issuance of bad checks, default on obligations and other similar occurrences or circumstances, including in any Sicredi entity, or having a negative record in any databases; VIII - not have, through intent or fault, committed any irregularity that has caused any damage or damage to Sicredi's image in any of Sicredi's member entities; IX - have operated assiduously and regularly with the Cooperative in the last two fiscal years (being an active member) and having participated in at least 2 (two) core assemblies in the last 3 (three) fiscal years; X - have participated in the exercise immediately Anti, or participate in the courses and other programming and recycling events programmed by the system, according to the nature of the position or function, without prejudice to the attendance to all the other events directed to the counselors during the period of his mandate, until the period of 1 (one) year after taking office; XI - have availability of time for the integral to the statutory and legal incumbency, being prohibited the accumulation with another position the function (elective or not), which requires dedication incompatible with responsibility in the operative; XII - preferably be exercising or have exercised, as holder or substitute, the role of Center Coordinator of the Belonging Program in the Cooperative where he is a member, or have held a statutory position in the cooperative; XIII - have certification from the Crescer Program; XIV - meet other requirements arising from the relevant legislation; XV - Do not expose negatively, or to directly or indirectly attempt the image of any entities of Sicredi, its statutory meters or employees, due to the position, by any means, verbal or written, or any marks of ownership the entities that make up Sicredi; XVI - not having suffered a sanction for infraction of extremely serious nature, in accordance with Sicredi's Internal Regulations.
