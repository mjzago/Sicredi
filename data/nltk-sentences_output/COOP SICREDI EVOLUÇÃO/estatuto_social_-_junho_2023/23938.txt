The surpluses determined at the end of each year (consolidated results) will be allocated as follows: I - 45% (forty-five percent), at least, to the Reserve Fund, intended to repair losses and to attend to the development of the Cooperative; II – 5% (five percent) at least, for the Technical, Educational and Social (FATES), intended to provide assistance to members, their families and Cooperative employees; III – recomposition of resources from the guarantee funds, in accordance with current legislation.
