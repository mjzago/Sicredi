The Executive Director of Banco Cooperativo Sicredi is responsible for: • Ensuring the effective local or regional implementation of this Policy and the actions aimed at its effectiveness; • Monitor local or regional performance and implemented actions, directing improvement whenever necessary; • Advise the Entity's Board of Directors within the scope of this Policy; • Ensure the correct disclosure of this Policy and other relevant information; • Represent the Entity before the Central Bank for issues related to this Policy.
