Regulation – Belonging Classification of Information – [Classification] Page 8 of 10 Classification of information: Internal Use § 1º Constitute hypotheses of vacancy of core coordinators: I - loss of membership status; II - failure to attend, without justification, the general meeting of the cooperative, at the discretion of the Board of Directors; III - death, resignation and dismissal; IV - health reasons, when these make it impossible for the coordinator to actively participate in their duties; V - as a party or attorney-in-fact, promote legal action against the cooperative itself or any other entity that is part of Sicredi, except those that aim to protect the exercise of their own mandate; VI - no longer meets the conditions for the role of nucleus coordinator, in accordance with these Regulations; VII - be elected a member of the Board of Directors or Supervisory Board of the cooperative, or even a director; VIII - hold a position on corporate bodies in a credit cooperative other than Sicredi; IX – the transfer of the coordinator, under any circumstances, to another nucleus; and X - become an employee of another cooperative in the Sicredi or financial institution.
