Inflows and expenditures, as well as revenues and expenses, are recorded monthly in accordance with the accrual basis, which establishes that inflows and expenditures and income and expenses must be included in the calculation of results for the periods in which they occur, whenever simultaneously when they correlate, regardless of receipt or payment, allocated proportionally according to the amounts of gross income from a cooperative act and gross revenue from a non-cooperative act, when not identified with each activity.
