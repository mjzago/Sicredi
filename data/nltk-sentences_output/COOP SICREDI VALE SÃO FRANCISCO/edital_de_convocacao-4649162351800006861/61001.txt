The Assembly will be held digitally for all members, using the Digital Assembly platform accessed through the website www.sicredi.com.br/assembleiadigital, to deliberate on the following matters, which make up the agenda: 1) Proposal for broad reform of the Cooperative's Bylaws; 2) Approval of the consolidation of the Bylaws; 3) Assessment and deliberation on the creation of the Social Fund; 4) Assessment of the Expansion and Development Reserve Regulation.
