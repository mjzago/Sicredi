f) Provision for losses expected losses associated with credit risk Provision for expected losses associated with credit risk is based on the analysis of operations, taking into account the economic situation, past experience, the specific and global risks of the portfolios, considering the provisioning criteria, defined in Resolutions nº 2,682/99 and nº 2,697/00 of the CMN, associated with assessments carried out by the Administration, in determining credit risks.
