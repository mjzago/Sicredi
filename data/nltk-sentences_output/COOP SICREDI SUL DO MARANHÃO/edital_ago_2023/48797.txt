The Assembly will be held digitally for all members, using the Digital Assembly platform accessed through the website www.sicredi.com.br/assembleiadigital, to deliberate on the following matters, which make up the agenda: 1) Consideration of the Management Report, General Balance Sheet and Accounting Statements, accompanied by the Opinion of the Supervisory Board relating to the 2022 financial year; 2) Ratiﬁcation of the Senior Management Position Succession Policy; 3) Assessment and deliberation of the Remuneration Policy for Central Sicredi Nordeste Executive Directors and its Affiliates approved in February 2023; 4) Classification in zone 02 (two) of the value of the Pre-Labour of the Executive Board and the President of the Board of Directors, as well as payment of the Christmas bonus in the amount of 01 (one) Pro—|abore/month, according to the Sicredi System remuneration table and the value of the Attendance Certificate of the Board of Directors and the Fiscal Council; 5) Allocation of Results for the year 2022.
