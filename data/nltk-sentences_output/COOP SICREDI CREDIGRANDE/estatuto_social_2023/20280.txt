The surpluses determined at the end of each year (consolidated result) will be allocated as follows: I - 55% (fifty-five percent), at least, to the reserve fund, intended to repair losses and meet the development of the Cooperative; II - 5% (five percent), at least, for the Technical Assistance Fund, Educational and Social (FATES), aimed at providing assistance to members, their families, Cooperative employees and the community located in its area of ​​action; III - the remaining balance will be made available to the general assembly, for purposes it deems appropriate, in compliance with the provisions of § 1 of this article.
