The elimination of a member will be decided by the Cooperative's Board of Directors and the reason must be included in its records, due to: I - Violation of current legislation or the Statute, when exclusion is not applicable; II - if the member fails to promptly comply with obligations and other commitments assumed with the Cooperative for up to 179 (one hundred and seventy-nine) days; Classification of information: Unrestricted Use III - violation of Sicredi's internal regulations; IV - practice of acts that characterize reckless or fraudulent management, as an administration advisor or director.
