The Board of Directors is governed by the following rules: I - meets, ordinarily, at least every 2 (two) months, and extraordinarily whenever necessary, upon call by its President or the majority of the Board itself ; II - deliberates, validly, by a simple majority of votes, with the majority of its components present, with the President having the tie-breaking vote; III - the deliberations of the C olegiate and other Substantial occurrences at meetings will be recorded in minutes, approved and signed by the members present.
