SANTA CATARINA - With the aim of building and experiencing attitudes and values ​​of cooperation and citizenship, through cooperative education practices, Sicredi Programa A União Faz a Vida Aliança RS/SC/ES promoted, on the 17th and 18th of March, in the hall of the Evangelical Church, a training session of the A União Faz a Vida Program for teachers from seven municipal schools in Alfredo Wagner (SC).
