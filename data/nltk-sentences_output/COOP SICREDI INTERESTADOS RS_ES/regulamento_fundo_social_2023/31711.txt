FRAMEWORK FOR PROJECT REGISTRATION To be able to register projects, entities must meet the following criteria: Entity: • Be registered with the CNPJ, with regular and active, and have, expressed in the statute or constitutive act, at least one of the following listed purposes, which are education, culture, sport, health, environment, security and social inclusion; • Be in compliance with obligations to the Cooperative, if the entity is already associated with Sicredi Interestates RS/ES; • Not belonging to and/or promoting actions of a partisan political nature.
