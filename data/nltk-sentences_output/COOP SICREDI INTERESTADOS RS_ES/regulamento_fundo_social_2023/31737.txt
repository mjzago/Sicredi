From the result of each year (before allocations), 1.5% will be distributed, and must be assessed and approved by the General Assembly, in the following proportion between agencies: • 40% apportioned to the results of each agency; • 20% prorated by the total number of members of each agency; • 20% prorated by the increase in associates (in the year) in each agency; • 20% prorated for use of the Sicredi credit card; A minimum value of R$8,000.00 per agency will be considered.
