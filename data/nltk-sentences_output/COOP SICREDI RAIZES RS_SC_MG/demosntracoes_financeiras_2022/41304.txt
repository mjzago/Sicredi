Inflows and expenditures, as well as income and expenses, are recorded monthly in accordance with the competence, which establishes that inflows and expenditures and revenues and expenses must be included in the calculation of the results of the periods in which they occur, always simultaneously when they correlate, regardless of receipt or payment, allocated proportionally in accordance with the amounts of gross income from a cooperative act and gross revenue from a non-cooperative act, when not identified with each activity.
