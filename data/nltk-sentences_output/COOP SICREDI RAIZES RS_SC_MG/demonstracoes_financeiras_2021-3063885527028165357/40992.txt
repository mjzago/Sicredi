Banco Cooperativo Sicredi is responsible for the set of policies, strategies and methodologies aimed at controlling and managing exposures to credit risk of the companies that make up the System, with the main duties being: responding to corporate credit risk management policies; develop and propose methodologies for credit risk classification, including through quantitative models; assess and control capital requirements to cover assumed credit risk; and carry out constant monitoring of exposures subject to credit risk of all Sicredi companies.
