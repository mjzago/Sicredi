In order to share the knowledge acquired throughout 2021 in the activities of the A União Faz a Vida Program, teachers and students from EMEI Favo de schools Mel, EMEI Criança Feliz, EMEF Hygino Coelho Portella, EMEI Tio Luiz, EMEF Honorino Pereira Borges and EMEI Sonho de Criança, from Marau, and Escola Frederico Benvegnú, from São Domingos do Sul, promoted, between the 6th and 9th of December , Pedagogical Exhibitions in its schools, covering various topics such as the environment, health, healthy eating, respect for diversity, dialogue, solidarity, among other subjects.
