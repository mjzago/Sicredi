Processes for risk management socio-environmental aspects of the Sicredi system include o Internal standardization containing rules, methodologies and responsibilities regarding the management of the topic; o Monitoring delimitations and fences of exposures subject to socio-environmental risk, in line with the system's risk appetite: o Collection and use of data to measure, classify and evaluate social, environmental and climate risks in operations; o Periodic performance of stress tests for socio-environmental risk scenarios; c linterlocution and reporting to environmental agencies, federations, business partners and governance forums; o Constant evolution of the note strategy, aiming for alignment with market techniques and technologies, as well as the expectations of interested parties.
