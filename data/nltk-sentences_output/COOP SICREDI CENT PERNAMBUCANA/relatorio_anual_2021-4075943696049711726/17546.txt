The processes for managing the Sicredi System's Iquidity risk include the Definition of processes to identify , evaluate, monitor and control exposure to liquidity risk in different time horizons; a The establishment of processes for tracking and reporting compliance with the liquidity risk appetite and at levels considered acceptable by the institution; a Definition of fundraising strategies that provide adequate diversification of funding sources and maturity periods: a Definition of an Iquidity contingency plan, regularly updated, which establishes responsibilities and procedures to face situations of liquidity stress a Periodic performance of stress tests with scenarios of short and long term.
