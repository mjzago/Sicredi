The processes for managing the IRRBB risk of the Sicredi System includes Procedures designed to measure, monitor and maintain exposure to IRRBB risk at levels considered acceptable by the Institution; a Processes aimed at monitoring and reporting adherence to the Institution's IRRBB risk appetite in relation to its capital: a Definition of the IRRBB risk methodologies to be applied; a Systems to perform the calculation and measure risks, considering the complexity of the products and the extent of exposure to the risk of IRRBB of the System's institutions.
