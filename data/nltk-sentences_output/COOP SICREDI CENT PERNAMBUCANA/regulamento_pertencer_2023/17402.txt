§ 1° The hypotheses of vacancy of nucleoz coordinators I - loss of membership status; II - failure to attend, without justification, the general meeting of the cooperative, at the discretion of the Board of Directors; III - death, resignation and destitution; IV - health reasons, when these make it impossible for the coordinator to actively participate in his duties; V - as a party or attorney-in-fact, promote legal action against the cooperative itself or any other entity that is part of Sicredi, except those that aim to protect the exercise of its own mandate; VI - no longer meets the conditions for the role of core coordinator, in accordance with this Regulation; VII - be elected member of the Administrative Council or Fiscal Council of the cooperative, or even director; VIII - hold a position in corporate bodies in a credit cooperative other than Sicredi; IX — the transfer of the coordinator, under any circumstances, to another nucleus; and X - become an employee of another Sicredi cooperative or financial institution.
