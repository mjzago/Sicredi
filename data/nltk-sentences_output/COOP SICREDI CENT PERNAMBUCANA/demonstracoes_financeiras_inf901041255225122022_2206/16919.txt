The Sicredi System defines the rules for calculating the risk of variation in the interest rate of operations in Is in line with market practices and the requirements of current regulations The processes for managing IRRBB risk in the Sicredi System include: 0 Procedures designed to measure, monitor and maintain exposure to IRRBB risk at levels considered acceptable by the Institution; 0 Processes designed to monitor and report adherence to the institution's IRRBB risk appetite in relation to its capital; 0 Definition of the IRRBB risk methodologies to be applied; 0 Systems to perform the calculation and measure risks, considering the complexity of the products and the extent of exposure to the IRRBB risk of the System's institutions.
