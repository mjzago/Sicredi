§ 59 The following constitute, among others, hypotheses of vacancy in an elected position: I — loss of associate status; II — death, resignation and dismissal, at any time, in accordance with current legislation; Ill ~ licenses, absences, suspensions or impediments equal to or greater than 120 (one hundred and twenty) days; IV - become the ineligible holder in accordance with the regulations in force, or no longer meet the basic conditions for holding an elected position under the terms of this Statute.
