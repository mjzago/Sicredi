General meetings (ordinary and/or extraordinary) will normally be called by the President of the Board of Directors, at least 10 (ten) days in advance, in a triple and cumulative manner, in a single publication, through a notice published in a newspaper and posted in appropriate locations on the premises most frequently frequented by members, in addition to communication through physical circulars or electronic means, obeying the following quorum for installation: I - 2/3 (two thirds) of the number of members, on first call; II — half plus 1 (one) of the number of members, on second call; lll — 10 (ten) members, in the third and final call.
