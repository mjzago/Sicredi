The Board of Directors is governed by the following rules: I v reﬁne, monthly in an ordinary manner, and extraordinarily whenever necessary, at the request of the Chairman of the Board of Directors, the majority of the Board of Directors itself, the Executive Board, or even at the request of the Fiscal Council; II - deliberates, validly, by a simple majority of votes, with the majority of its components present, with the Chairman of the Board of Directors having the tie-breaking vote; lll — the Council's deliberations and other substantial occurrences at the meetings will be recorded in detailed Minutes, drawn up in the proper Book, read, approved and signed, at the end of the work, by the members of the Board of Directors present.
