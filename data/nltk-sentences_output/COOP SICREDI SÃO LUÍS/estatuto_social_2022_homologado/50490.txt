§ 19 0 Cooperative Credit System — Sicredi or System is the set of individual Credit Cooperatives, their respective Central Cooperatives, the Confederation of Sicredi Cooperatives (confederagﬁo Sicredi), Sicredi Participagoes S/A (SicrediPar) and all legal entities in which they participate directly or indirectly, the Foundation for Educational and Cultural Development of the Cooperative Credit System ( Fundagﬁo Sicredi) and Sicredi Fundo Garantidores (SFG).
