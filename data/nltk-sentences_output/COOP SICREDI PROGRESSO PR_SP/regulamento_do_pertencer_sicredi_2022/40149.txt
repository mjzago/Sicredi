The following will be considered elected nucleus coordinators: I - the member with the most votes and the second most voted member by the nucleus will respectively be considered effective coordinator and substitute coordinator, and so on according to the greater number of substitutes; II - the members with the highest number of votes, when presented as candidates jointly (permanent and substitute(s)), indicating the order of substitutes; III - the members presented as the only candidates (effective and substitute(s)), acclaimed by the Core Assembly.
