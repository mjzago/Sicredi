The surpluses determined at the end of each year (consolidated result) will be allocated as follows: I - 70% (seventy percent), at least, to the reserve fund, intended to repair losses and meet development of the Cooperative; II - 5% (five percent), at least, to the Technical, Educational and Social Assistance Fund (FATES), intended for providing assistance to associates, their family members, Cooperative employees and the community located in its area of ​​action; III - recomposition of resources from the guarantee funds, in accordance with current legislation; IV - the remaining balance will be made available to the general assembly, for purposes it deems appropriate, in compliance with the provisions of § 1 of this article.
