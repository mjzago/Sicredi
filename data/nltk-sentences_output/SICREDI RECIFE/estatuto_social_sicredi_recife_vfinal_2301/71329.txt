The surplus determined at the end of each year (consolidated result) will be allocated as follows: I - 10% (ten percent), at least, gradually increasing by 3% (three percent) each year, up to a limit of 45 % (forty-five percent) for the reserve fund, intended to repair losses and support the development of the Cooperative; II - 5% (five percent), at least, for the Technical, Educational and Social Assistance Fund (FATES), intended for the provision of assistance to members, their families, Cooperative employees and the community located in its area of ​​action; III - the remaining balance will be made available to the general assembly, for purposes it deems appropriate, in compliance with the provisions of § 1 of this article.
