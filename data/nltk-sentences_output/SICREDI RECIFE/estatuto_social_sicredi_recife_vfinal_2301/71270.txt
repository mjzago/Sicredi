The Board of Directors is governed by the following rules: I - meets, monthly in an ordinary manner, and extraordinarily whenever necessary, upon call by its President, the majority of the Board itself, or, even, by request the Fiscal Council; II - deliberates, validly, by majority simple vote, with the majority of its members present, with the President having the tie-breaking vote; III - the deliberations of the Board and other substantial occurrences at the meetings will be recorded in minutes, approved and signed by the members present, the content of which the President of the Board must also inform the Cooperative's Supervisory Board promptly.
