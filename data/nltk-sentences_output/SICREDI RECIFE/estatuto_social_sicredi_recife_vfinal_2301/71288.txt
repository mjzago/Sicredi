The Executive Board is responsible, without prejudice to the responsibilities provided for in legislation and internal regulations: I - operationally administer the Cooperative, taking into account its purpose, the guidelines issued by the General Assembly, the Board of Directors and corporate guidelines and strategies from Sicredi; II - contract obligations, compromise, sign agreements in legal proceedings, collective agreements or conventions, give in and compromise or waive rights, as well as monitor the economic and financial state of the company, in compliance with the provisions of these Bylaws; Classification of information: Internal Use III - appoint attorneys, establishing for them, in an instrument of skillful mandate, duties, powers and responsibilities and form of representation, which may be isolated or jointly, within the limits of this Statute.
