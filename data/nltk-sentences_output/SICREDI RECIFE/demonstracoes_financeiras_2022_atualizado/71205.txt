EXPLANATORY NOTES TO THE FINANCIAL STATEMENTS (IN THOUSANDS OF REAIS) Income and expenditure, as well as income and expenses, are recorded monthly in accordance with the accrual basis, which establishes that income and expenditure, income and expenses must be included in the calculation of the results of periods in which they occur, always simultaneously when they are correlated, regardless of receipt or payment, allocated proportionally according to the amounts of the gross input of the cooperative act and the gross revenue from non-cooperative acts, when not identified with each activity.
