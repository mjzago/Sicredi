Nucleus coordinators will be considered elected: I - the member with the most votes and the second most voted by the nucleus will respectively be considered effective coordinator and alternate coordinator, and so on as there is a greater number of substitutes; II - the members with the highest number of votes, when presented as candidates jointly (effective and alternate(s)), the order of substitute(s) indicated; III - the members presented as the only candidate acts (effective and substitute(s)), acclaimed by the Core Assembly.
