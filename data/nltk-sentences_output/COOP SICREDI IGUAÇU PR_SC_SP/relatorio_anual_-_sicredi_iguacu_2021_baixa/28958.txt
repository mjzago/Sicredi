The Sicredi associates included were Kelly Lima, from the Sicredi Alta Noroeste SP cooperative, for her support in the construction of the Women's Guide, addressing the rights of women suffering from cancer and victims of domestic violence; Rafael Lopes, from Sicredi Paranapanema PR/SP, with the “Let’s Help Those Who Need It Most” campaign, encouraging donations in support for victims of a strong windstorm in their region; Guilherme de Melo, also from Sicredi Paranapanema, with his participation in a project that supported the distribution of soap to needy families in his community.
