In 2022, our cooperative reached the milestone of 70 thousand members and, strengthening our physical presence, we focused on expanding our service network: two more branches in Campinas (SP), in addition to the reopening of new spaces in Dois Vizinhos (PR), Itapejara D'Oeste (PR), Nova Prata do Iguaçu (PR), Verê (PR) and São Lourenço do Oeste (SC).
