Nucleus coordinators will be considered elected: I - the member with the most votes and the second most voted by the nucleus will respectively be considered effective coordinator and substitute coordinator, and so on depending on the number of substitutes; II - the members with the highest number of votes, when presented as candidates jointly (effective and alternate(s)), the order of substitute(s) indicated; III - the members presented as the only candidate acts (effective and substitute(s)), acclaimed by the Core Assembly.
