Non-banking financial institution, authorized to operate by the Central Bank of Brazil, which began its activities on 09/05/1989 and its main objectives are: i) Developing savings programs, appropriate use of credit and provision of services, carrying out all active, passive and accessory operations typical of credit cooperatives; ii) Provide, through mutuality, financial assistance to members in their specific activities; iii) Work on the educational training of its members, with a view to promoting cooperativism.
