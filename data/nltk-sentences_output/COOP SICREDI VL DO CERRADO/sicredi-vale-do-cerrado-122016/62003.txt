The processes for managing the Sicredi System's liquidity risk include: • Definition of processes to identify, assess, monitor and control exposure to liquidity risk over different time horizons; • Establishment of operational limits to maintain adequate and sufficient levels of liquidity; • Definition of funding strategies that provide adequate diversification of funding sources and maturity dates; •Definition of a liquidity contingency plan, regularly updated, that establishes responsibilities and procedures to deal with liquidity stress situations; • Periodic carrying out stress tests with short and long-term scenarios.
