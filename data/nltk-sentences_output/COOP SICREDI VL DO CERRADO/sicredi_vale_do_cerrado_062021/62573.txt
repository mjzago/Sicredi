p) Taxes and contributions The Income Tax (IRPJ) and the Social Contribution on Net Profit (CSLL) affect the positive result in non-cooperative acts; In this case, the provision corresponds to the current rates for IRPJ (15%, plus an additional 10%) and CSLL (15%), applied to the profit earned, after the calculation base adjustments provided for in the legislation and the compensation of losses tax and negative CSLL calculation base from previous years, this deduction being limited to 30% of taxable profit.
