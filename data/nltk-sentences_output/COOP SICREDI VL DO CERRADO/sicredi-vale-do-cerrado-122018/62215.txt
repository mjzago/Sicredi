The Sicredi System's liquidity risk management processes include: • Definition of processes to identify, evaluate, monitor and control exposure to liquidity risk over different time horizons; • The establishment of processes for tracking and reporting compliance with the liquidity risk appetite set out in the RAS; • Definition of fundraising strategies that provide adequate diversification of funding sources and maturity dates; •Definition of a liquidity contingency plan, regularly updated, that establishes responsibilities and procedures to face liquidity stress situations; • Periodic carrying out stress tests with short and long-term scenarios.
