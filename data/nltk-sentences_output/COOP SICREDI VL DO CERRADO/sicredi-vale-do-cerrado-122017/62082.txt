The Sicredi System's market risk management processes include: • Clear rules for classifying the trading portfolio that guarantee the correct handling of operations; • • Procedures designed to measure, monitor and maintain exposure to market risk at levels considered acceptable by the Institution; • Operational limits that define the market risk tolerance of the System Entities in relation to their capital; • Definition of market risk methodologies to be applied; • Systems to perform the calculation and measure risks, considering the complexity of the products and the dimension of exposure to the risk of market of System Entities.NOTE 23 – RISK MANAGEMENT The Sicredi System considers risk management to be a priority in the conduct of its business activities, adopting practices in absolute accordance with the precepts of the Basel Accords.
