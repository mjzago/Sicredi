Processes for managing liquidity risk in the Sicredi System include: • Definition of processes to identify, evaluate, monitor and control exposure to liquidity risk over different time horizons; • Establishment of operational limits to maintain adequate levels and sufficient liquidity; • Definition of funding strategies that provide adequate diversification of funding sources and maturity dates; •Definition of a liquidity contingency plan, regularly updated, that establishes responsibilities and procedures to face situations of liquidity stress; • Periodic carrying out stress tests with short and long-term scenarios.
