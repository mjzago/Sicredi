The Board of Directors is governed by the following rules: I - meets, on an ordinary monthly basis, and extraordinarily whenever necessary, at the call of its President, the majority of its own Collegiate, or even at the request of the Supervisory Board; II - deliberates, validly, by a simple majority of votes, with the majority of its components present, with the President having the tie-breaking vote; Ill - the deliberations of the Board and other substantial occurrences at the meetings will be included in wings.
