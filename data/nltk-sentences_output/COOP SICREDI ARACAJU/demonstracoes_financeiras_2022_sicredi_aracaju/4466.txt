EXPLANATORY NOTES TO THE FINANCIAL STATEMENTS (IN THOUSANDS OF REAIS) Income and expenditure, as well as income and expenses, are recorded monthly in accordance with the accrual regime, which establishes that income and expenditure and income and expenses must be included in the calculation of the results of the periods in which they occur, always simultaneously when they are correlated, regardless of receipt or payment, allocated in a proportional according to the amounts of gross income from a cooperative act and gross revenue from a non-cooperative act, when not identified with each activity.
