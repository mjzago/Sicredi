EXPLANATORY NOTES TO THE FINANCIAL STATEMENTS (IN THOUSANDS OF REAIS) Income and expenditure, as well as income and expenses, are recorded monthly in accordance with the accrual basis, which establishes that income and expenditure and income and expenses must be included in the calculation of results of the periods in which they run, always simultaneously when they correlate, regardless of receipt or payment, allocated proportionally according to the amounts of the gross entry of the cooperative act and the gross revenue from non-cooperative acts, when not identified with each activity.
