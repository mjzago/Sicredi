The cooperative has branches in Rio Grande do Sul, in the municipalities of Erebango, Estação, Floriano Peixoto, Getúlio Vargas, Ipiranga do Sul, Jacutinga, Quatro Irmãos and Sertão, and in Minas Gerais, covering 45 municipalities, already has branches in the cities of Divinópolis, Pimenta, Piumhi, Santo Antônio do Monte and São Sebastião do Oeste.
