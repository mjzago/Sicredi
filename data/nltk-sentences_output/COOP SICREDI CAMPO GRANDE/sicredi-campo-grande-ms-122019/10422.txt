Non-banking financial institution, authorized to operate by the Central Bank of Brazil, which began its activities on 10/29/1998 and its main objectives are: i) Developing savings programs, appropriate use of credit and provision of services, carrying out all active and passive operations and accessories specific to credit unions; ii) Provide, through mutuality, financial assistance to members in their specific activities; iii) Work on the educational training of its members, with a view to promoting cooperativism.
