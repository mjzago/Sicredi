In the Legal Entity segment, there were highlights the credit lines such as the National Support Program for Micro and Small Businesses (PRONAMPE) and the Emergency Credit Access Program (PEAC), created on an emergency basis by the federal government in 2020, during the pandemic period, and which are still available to MEIs, micro and small companies in the case of PRONAMPE and additionally to medium-sized companies in the case of PEAC/FGI.
