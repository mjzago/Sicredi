The training took place in person at Cooperative branches, associated companies and on the official social networks of Sicredi Campo Grande MS; With the “União faz a Vida Program” implemented in Bandeirantes, we served 1,060 children and 130 teachers, from six schools the municipal education network of the municipality; In the month of September, the Sicredi Campo Grande Women's Committee promoted 13 Workshops for all employees, leading to reflection and awareness about the importance of human beings paying attention to their emotional health as a basis for proper quality of life and constant personal development.
