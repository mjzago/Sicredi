(c) Cash and cash equivalents and marketable securities in the cash flow statements Additionally, reclassifications were identified related to the change in the decentralization model where Cooperatives began to invest part of their short-term resources in fixed income and multimarket funds, which have immediate convertibility into a known amount of cash and are subject to an insignificant risk of value, making it possible to classify them as cash and cash equivalent in accordance with the premises of CPC 03.
