Inflows and expenditures, as well as revenues and expenses, are recorded monthly in accordance with the accrual regime, which establishes that inflows and expenditures funds and revenues and expenses must be included in the calculation of the results of the periods in which they occur, always simultaneously when they are correlated, regardless of receipt or payment, allocated proportionally according to the amounts of gross income from a cooperative act and gross revenue from a non-cooperative act, when not identified with each activity.
