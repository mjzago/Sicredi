The member, an individual, who reaches the age of 70 and, cumulatively, has been part of the Cooperative's membership for at least 05 (five) years or retires due to permanent disability, regardless of age, may, exceptionally, submit to the Board of Administration a request to withdraw part of its share capital, every 12 (twelve) month interval, maintaining its membership status, with a minimum of 1,000 (one thousand) shares.
