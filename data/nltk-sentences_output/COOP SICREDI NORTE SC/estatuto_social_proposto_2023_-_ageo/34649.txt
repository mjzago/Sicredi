The elimination of a member will be decided by the Board of Directors of the Cooperative and the reason must be included in its records, due to: I - Violation of current legislation or the Statute, when exclusion is not applicable ; II - if the member fails to promptly fulfill the obligations and other commitments assumed with the Cooperative within 179 (center and seventy-nine) days; III - violation of the internal regulations of the Sicredi; IV - carrying out acts that characterize reckless or fraudulent management, as a board member, supervisor or director.
