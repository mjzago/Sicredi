The following will be considered as core coordinators: I - the member with the most votes and the second most voted by the nucleus, they will be respectively considered effective coordinator and substitute coordinator, and so on depending on the greater number of substitutes; II - the members with the highest number of votes, when presented as candidates jointly (effective and substitute(s)), indicating the order of replacement; III - the members presented as the only candidates (effective and substitute(s)), acclaimed by the Core Assembly.
