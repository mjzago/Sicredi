In addition to other hypotheses provided for by law, the Cooperative dissolves itself in its own right: I - when the general assembly so decides, as long as 20 (twenty) members, at least, are not willing to ensure its continuity; II - for the amendment its legal form; III - by reducing the number of members, to less than 20 (twenty), or their minimum share capital, until the subsequent general assembly, held within a period of no less than 6 (six) months, are not reestablished; IV- by canceling the authorization to operate; V-by stopping their normal activities for more than 120 (one hundred and twenty) days.
