Among the actions to combat the pandemic, we highlight: a) Creation of special credit lines for the pandemic; b) Extension of loan maturities in order to adapt the associates' cash flow; c) Review of pricing in the various credit lines; d) Donations to entities in all municipalities; e) Campaigns to raise funds for those most in need; f) Encouragement of local commerce, including among employees; g) Periodic virtual meetings; g)Relaxation incentives through virtual interactive activities; h) Virtual meeting with all employees and family members at the end of the year.
