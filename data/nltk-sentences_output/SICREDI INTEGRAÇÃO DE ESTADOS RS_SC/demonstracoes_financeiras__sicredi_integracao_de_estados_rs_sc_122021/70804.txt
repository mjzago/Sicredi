Banco Cooperativo Sicredi is responsible for the set of policies, strategies and methodologies aimed at control and management of credit risk exposures of the companies that make up the System, with the following main duties: responding to corporate credit risk management policies; develop and propose credit risk classification methodologies, including through quantitative models; assess and control capital requirements to cover assumed credit risk; and carry out constant monitoring of exposures subject to credit risk of all companies in the Sicredi.
