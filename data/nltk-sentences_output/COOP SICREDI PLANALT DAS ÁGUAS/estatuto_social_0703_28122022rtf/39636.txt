The surplus determined at the end of each year (consolidated result) will be allocated as follows: I - 70% (seventy percent), at least, to the reserve fund, intended for repair losses and support the development of the Cooperative; II – 10% (ten percent), at least, to the Technical, Educational and Social Assistance Fund (FATES), intended to provide assistance to members, their families, employees of the Cooperative and the community located in their area of action; III - recomposition of resources from the guarantee funds, in accordance with current legislation; IV - the remaining balance will be made available to the general assembly, for purposes that deemed appropriate, in compliance with the provisions of § 1 of this article.
