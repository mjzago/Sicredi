§ 19 The Cooperative Credit System — Sicredi or System is the set of singular Credit Cooperatives, their respective Central Cooperatives, the Confederation of Sicredi Cooperatives (Confederation; §o Sicredi), Sicredi Participagoes S/A (SicrediPar) and all the legal entities in which they participate directly or indirectly, the Foundation for Educational and Cultural Development of the Cooperative Credit System (Fundagéo Sicredi) and Sicredi Fundo Garantidores (SFG).
