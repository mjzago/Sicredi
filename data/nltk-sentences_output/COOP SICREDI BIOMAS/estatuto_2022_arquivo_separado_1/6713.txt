§ 79 For the purposes of this Statute, a political-partisan position is understood as: I - Elective post - those political agents invested in their positions through electoral processes (Councillor, Mayor, State and Federal Deputy, Senator, Governor, President of the Repifiblica), in accordance with current electoral and constitutional legislation; ll - Member of a party executive — people who, affiliated with a specific party, are elected to occupy executive positions in the party.
