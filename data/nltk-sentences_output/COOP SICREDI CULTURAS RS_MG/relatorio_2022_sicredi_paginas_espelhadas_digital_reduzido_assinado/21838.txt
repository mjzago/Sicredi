Ijuí/RS With the aim of encouraging the use of non-polluting transport vehicles, which promote health, social well-being and quality of life, such as bicycles, scooters, roller skates and skateboards, we at Sicredi das Culturas RS/MG, together with the cooperatives from Ijuí/RS: Ceriluz, Cotripal and Unimed Noroeste/RS promote the action “Take Cooperation to Garupa” to celebrate C Day.
