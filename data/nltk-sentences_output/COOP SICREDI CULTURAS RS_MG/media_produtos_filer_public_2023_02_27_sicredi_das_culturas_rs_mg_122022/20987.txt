The processes for managing the IRRBB risk of the Si credi System include: • Procedures designed to measure, monitor and maintain exposure to risk of IRRBB at levels considered acceptable by the Institution; • Processes designed to monitor and report adherence to the Institution's IRRBB risk appetite in relation to its capital; • Definition of the IRRBB risk methodologies to be applied; • Systems to perform the calculation and measure risks, considering the complexity of the products and the size of the BB IRR risk exposure of the System's institutions.
