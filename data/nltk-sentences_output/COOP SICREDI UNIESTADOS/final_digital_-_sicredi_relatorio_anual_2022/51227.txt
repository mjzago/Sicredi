SELF-SUFFICIENCY Sicredi UniEstados, committed to building a more prosperous society, invested in its own photovoltaic solar plants, with power to generate more than one thousand two hundred kilowatts peak hour (1200kWp), making the cooperative self-sufficient in generating energy to supply its 28 branches in the Rio Grande do Sul and also its 21 agencies in the state of Santa Catarina, as well as its Administrative Headquarters.
