Vale Litoral SC, using the powers conferred on it by article 14 of the Bylaws, summons the napleo delegates, who on this date total 41 (Forty-one), to meet in an ORDINARY GENERAL ASSEMBLY, to be held on the 30th (thirty) of March 2022, at 7 (seven) hours, in first call, with the presence of 2/3 (two thirds) of the delegates, in second call, at 8 (eight) hours, with the presence of half of the delegates plus one, and, in the third and final call, at 9 (nine) hours, with the presence of at least 10 (ten) delegates.
