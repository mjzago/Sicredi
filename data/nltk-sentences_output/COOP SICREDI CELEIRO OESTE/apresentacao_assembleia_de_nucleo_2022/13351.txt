To apply and carry out the activities of nucleus coordinator, the applicant must: I-have certification in the Crescer Program; II-make use of at least 42 (four two) cooperative financial solutions; IV-not respond, due to pending issues relating to protest of titles, legal charges, issuance of checks without funds, non-compliance with obligations and other similar occurrences or circumstances, in addition to not having a negative record in any database, external or from the cooperative itself, as well as having promoted or being promoting, as a party or proxy, legal action against the cooperative itself or any other entity that is part of Sicredi; Classification of information: Internal UseV -not be employed by the cooperative or be fired from the cooperative for just cause; and VI-not be a former advisor or former director who is subject to an internal investigation to investigate violations of legal or systemic norms during his term of office or who, for the same reasons, has been removed from office or resigned from the position for which he was elected.
