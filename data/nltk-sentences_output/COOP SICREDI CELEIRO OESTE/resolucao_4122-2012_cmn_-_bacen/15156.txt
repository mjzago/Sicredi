§ 1° The Central Bank of Brazil, prior to the cancellation referred to in the caput, must: I - disclose to the public, by means it deems most appropriate, your intention to cancel the authorization in question, with a view to the possible presentation of objections within a period of 30 (thirty) days; II - initiate administrative proceedings, notifying the institution at the address provided to the Central Bank of Brazil to express its opinion on the intention to cancel; III - consider the risks of cancellation for the stability of the national financial system, for popular savings and for the institution's operational creditors.
