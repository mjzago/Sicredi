The Central Bank of Brazil may, if it deems necessary, adopt the following measures regarding the statement of purposes referred to in the caput, both in isolated cases and through general rules and procedures: I - determine its publication, in the case of those elected or appointed to positions of member of the board of directors, director or partner-administrator and, also, in the case of those whose names have already been previously accepted by the Authority; ll - establish the form and the deadline for its publication, as well as the deadline for receiving objections from the public, with a view to the progress of the respective process; Ill - disseminate it through whatever means it deems most appropriate.
