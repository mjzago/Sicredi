§ 2° The Commission will be composed of, at least, O3 (three) associated natural persons, with the option of indicating substitutes, subject to the following requirements: a) Do not make up the list of candidates; b) Not holding a position on the Board of Directors, Fiscal Council or Executive Board of the Cooperative; c) Not be spouses, partners, relatives up to the 2nd (second) degree, straight or collateral, of the candidates or those holding current mandates on the Board of Directors, Supervisory Board or Executive Board of the Cooperative; d) Not be employees of the Cooperative.
