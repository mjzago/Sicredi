X - decide on the convening of the General Assembly; XI - previously authorize capital participation in other companies or entities, complying with the social purposes of the cooperative and respecting the current legislation and the enforcements and only guidelines on it; XII - authorize the change of the headquarters address, within the same municipality, as well as to deliberate about the opening, closing, transfer or change of address of other facilities or branches of the Cooperative, inside or outside the host municipality, in accordance with current legislation; XIII - comply with and enforce this Statute, the legislation and internal regulations of Sicredi; XIV – express opinions on the management report and financial statements for the year; XV - choose and dismiss external auditors, in compliance with systemic guidelines; XVI - deliberate on specific matters of interest to the Cooperative, as well as omitted cases and all other duties provided for in these Bylaws and in the relevant legislation, until subsequent deliberation by the General Assembly, if applicable; XVII – establish or terminate committees to meet the Cooperative’s needs, appointing and dismissing its members, in accordance with the Internal Regulations; XVIII – deliberate on policies and guidelines relating to internal controls, security and safety risk management and respective contingency plans, proposed by the Executive Board; XIX – decide on the application of the Technical, Educational and Social Assistance Fund – FATES; XX – prepare and submit to the decision of the General Assembly a proposal for the creation of funds; XXI – decide, on a discretionary basis, on the form and deadlines for returning quotas - shares of share capital referring to dismissed, excluded or eliminated members, provided that the projections of the result of the Cooperative at the time of release is positive and the redemption does not imply non-compliance with the operational limits defined by the regulatory body and by law; XXII – deliberate on the convening of General and Nucleus Assemblies; XXIII – remove delegate, in accordance with this Statute and other applicable rules; XXIV – define, organize and publicize, in a timely manner, the grouping of members into groups; XXV – establish rules for omitted cases, until subsequent deliberation of the General Assembly.
