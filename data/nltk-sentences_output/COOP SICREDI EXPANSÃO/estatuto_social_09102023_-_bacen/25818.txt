President and 10 (ten) members, constituting basic conditions for the candidacy and exercise of the position, without prejudice to meeting the additional requirements set out in systemic regulations: I - not maintain an employment relationship with any entity that is part of Sicredi or, even, with a member of the Board of Directors, Fiscal Council or Executive Board of the Cooperative; II - not be a spouse or partner, nor be related up to the 2nd (second) degree, directly or collaterally, with members of the Administration and Fiscal Councils and the Executive Board, as well as with employees of the Cooperative; III - not be, simultaneously, an employee or administrator or hold a stake in a company that, due to its activities, is considered to compete with any of the entities that make up Sicredi or whose capital these participate; IV - not have held or be holding a party political position or function in the last four years; V - meet the professional qualifications required for the position, in accordance with current legislation and in accordance with systemic regulations, compatible with the complexity of the inherent activities; VI - not having used 2 (two) or more debt renegotiations at the Cooperative or another Sicredi entity, in the last 2 (two) years; VII - do not respond or any company of which he is a controller or administrator, due to pending issues relating to protest of titles, legal charges, issuance of bad checks, default on obligations and other similar occurrences or circumstances, including in any Sicredi entity, or have a negative record in any banks data; VIII - not have, through intent or fault, committed an irregularity that has caused any damage or damage to the image of Sicredi or any of its entities members of Sicredi; IX - have worked assiduously and regularly with the Cooperative in the last two fiscal years (be an active member) and have participated in at least 2 (two) Ordinary Core Assemblies in the last 3 (three) fiscal years; X - have participated, in the immediately preceding exercise, or participate in the courses and other training and recycling events programmed by the system, according to the nature of the position or function, without prejudice to the attendance to all other events aimed at directors during their term of office, up to 1 (one) year after taking office; XI - have availability of time for full compliance with statutory and legal incumbency, being prohibited accumulation with another position or function (he or not), which requires dedication incompatible with responsibility in the cooperative;
