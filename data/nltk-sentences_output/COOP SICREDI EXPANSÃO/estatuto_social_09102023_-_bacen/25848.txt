The Administration Council is responsible, within the limits of the law and these Statutes: I - establish the strategic direction of the Cooperative and monitor the execution and fulfillment of the planning approved by it; II - monitor and supervise the performance of the Cooperative's Executive Board, in view of the objectives and goals defined for the Society; III - approve regulations within its competence, including the Internal Regulations, which cannot contradict the provisions of systemic regulations; IV - appoint and dismiss the members of the Executive Board, as well as establish their duties and responsibilities and the individual remuneration of the directors, observing the provisions contained in the Statute; V - authorize the contracting credit operations with financial institutions, intended to finance the activities of members, in compliance with the provisions of systemic regulations; VI - decide on the payment of the annual remuneration on the capital shares, stipulating the remuneration, in accordance with current legislation; VII - forward to the General Assembly a proposal for the donation of the Cooperative's real estate, classified as non-current; VIII - deliberate on the acquisition, alienation or encumbrance of the Cooperative's real estate, classified as non-current; This document was digitally signed by Edvaldo Maia Lopes Ferreira Filho, Maurilio Da Silva Ferraz, Ricardo Goncalves Tavares and Lenildo Amorim Da Silva.
