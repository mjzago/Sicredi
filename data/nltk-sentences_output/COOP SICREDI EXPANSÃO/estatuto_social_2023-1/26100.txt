III – The Business Director: a) coordinate Sicredi Expansão’s financial operations; b) defer, within the limits established by the Board of Directors, for its jurisdiction, general credit operations, as provided for in the Internal regulations; c) carry out activities related to financial functions; d) advise the Executive Director on matters within his area; e) together with the Executive Director or Operations Director, sign balance sheets and statements of surpluses and losses, loan and financing contracts and other documents pertinent to the administration and management of Sicredi Expansão; f) resolve omitted cases, together with the Executive Director; g) develop other duties that the Board of Directors and/or Internal Regulations confer on it.
