The Notices of the General Meetings must include: a) the name of the Cooperative, followed by the expression ORDINARY GENERAL MEETING OR/AND EXTRAORDINARY, as the case may be; b) the day and time of the meeting, in each call, as well as the place of its holding, which, unless there is a justified reason, it will always be the registered office; c) the ordinal sequence of calls; d) the agenda of the work with the appropriate specifications and, in the case of a Statute Reform, an indication of the matter; e) the number of delegates existing on the date of its issuance, for the purpose of calculating the installation quorum; f) place, date and signature of the person responsible for the call.
