The elimination of a member will be decided by the Executive Board of the Cooperative and the reason must be included in its records, due to: I - violation of current legislation or the Statute, when exclusion is not applicable; II - if the member fails to promptly fulfill the obligations and other commitments assumed with the Cooperative for up to 179 (one hundred and seventy-nine) days; III - violation of regulations internal at Sicredi; IV - practice of acts that characterize reckless or fraudulent management, as a Board Member, Auditor or Director.
