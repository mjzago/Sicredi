It is the responsibility of the Board of Directors, within the limits of the law and these Statutes: I - to establish the strategic direction of the Cooperative and monitor the execution and fulfillment of the planning approved by it; II - monitor and supervise the performance of the Executive Board of the Cooperative, in view of the objectives and goals defined for the Society; III - approve regulations within its competence, including the Internal Regulations, which cannot contradict the provisions of systemic regulations; IV - appoint and dismiss the members of the Executive Board, as well as establish their duties and responsibilities and the individual remuneration of the directors, observing the provisions contained in the Statute; V - authorize the contracting of credit operations with institutions financial, intended to finance the activities of members, in compliance with the provisions of systemic regulations; VI - decide on the payment of the annual remuneration on the capital shares, stipulating the remuneration, in accordance with current legislation; Classification of information: Internal Use VII - forward to the General Assembly a proposal for the donation of the Cooperative's real estate, classified as non-current; VIII - decide on the acquisition, disposal or encumbrance of the Cooperative's real estate, classified as non-current; IX - examine and investigate reports of infractions committed within the Company, including those sent to them by the Executive Board and the Fiscal Council, and determine the application of applicable penalties; X - decide on the convening of the General Assembly; XI - authorize, in advance, capital participation in other companies or entities, meeting the social purposes of the Cooperative and respected current legislation and systemic deliberations and guidelines in this regard; XII - authorize the change of the headquarters address, within the same municipality, as well as to decide on the opening, closing, transfer or change of address of the other facilities or branches of the Cooperative, within or outside the headquarters municipality, in the terms of current legislation; XIII - comply with and enforce this Statute, legislation and internal regulations of Sicredi; XIV – express opinions on the management report and financial statements for the year; XV - choose and dismiss external auditors, in compliance with systemic guidelines; XVI - deliberate on specific matters of interest to the Cooperative, as well as on omitted cases and all other duties provided for in these Bylaws and in the relevant legislation, until subsequent deliberation by the General Assembly, if applicable; XVII – establish or extinguish committees to meet the needs of the Cooperative, appointing and dismissing its members, in accordance with the Internal Regulations; XVIII – deliberate on the policies and guidelines relating to internal controls, security and risk management and respective contingency plans, proposed by the Executive Board; XIX – decide on the application of the Technical, Educational and Social Assistance Fund – FATES; XX – prepare and submit to the decision of the General Assembly a proposal for the creation of funds; XXI – decide, on a discretionary basis, on the form and deadlines for the return of shares of share capital relating to dismissed, excluded or eliminated members, provided that the projections of the Cooperative's results at the time of release are positive and the redemption does not imply non-compliance with the operational limits defined by the regulatory body and by law; XXII – decide on the convening of Nucleus Assemblies; XXIII – dismiss delegate, as per this Statute and other applicable rules; XXIV – define, organize and publicize, in a timely manner, the grouping of members into groups; Classification of information: Internal Use XXV – establish rules for omitted cases, until further deliberation by the General Assembly.
