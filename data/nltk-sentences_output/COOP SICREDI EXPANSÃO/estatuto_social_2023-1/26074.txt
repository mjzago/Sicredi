The Board of Directors is governed by the following rules: I – meets, ordinarily, once a month and, extraordinarily, whenever necessary, upon call by the President of the Board of Directors, the majority of the Board of Directors or the Supervisory Board; II – deliberates, validly, by a simple majority of votes, with the majority of its components present, with the President having the tie-breaking vote; III – the deliberations will be recorded in detailed, detailed minutes, with all the circumstances, drawn up in the appropriate book, read, approved and signed, at the end of the work, by the members of the Board of Directors present; IV – its deliberations will be incorporated into the Si credi Expansão Normative System.
