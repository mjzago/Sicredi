Along with the request for a protocol from each ticket/candidate, the applicants are responsible for delivering the documents for each component/candidate, listed below, the veracity of which will be verified by the Electoral Commission, through the means its scope: I certified copy of the identification document and CPF; II declaration of disqualification from exercising the position, if elected to the governing body administration or supervision; III negative or positive certificates with negative effects on debts of federal, state and municipal taxes and contributions, including those of any company of which he is the controller or administrator; IV civil, state and federal certificate, from their respective domiciles and where they carry out activity, issued with a date no later than 30 (thirty) days from the date established for delivery, including from any company of which they are controlling or administrator; V criminal, state and federal certificate of the respective domiciles, issued with a date no later than 30 (thirty) days from the date established for its delivery; VI electoral criminal certificate, issued with a date no later than 30 (thirty) days from the date established for its delivery; VII negative or positive certificate with negative effects on labor debts; VIII negative certificate of title protest; IX declaration issued by the candidate that he does not appear in the register of issuers of bad checks (CCF); X copy of the latest income tax declaration, or exemption declaration, in both cases with updated asset stratification; XI professional curriculum; XII form/declaration(s) provided by the Cooperative duly completed and signed by all members of the list(s)/candidate; XIII for candidates for the Supervisory Board, document proving that they have satisfactorily attended the course of fiscal council for credit cooperatives with a minimum duration of 16 (sixteen) hours and participation in at least 2/3 (two thirds) of the Ordinary General Assemblies, in the last 3 (three) years; XIV for candidates for the Board of Directors, document proving that they have satisfactorily attended the course(s) required in this code, and participation in at least 2/3 (two thirds) of the Ordinary General Assemblies in the last 3 (three) years; XV to candidates for the position of delegate, document proving that they have satisfactorily attended the course of the Sicredi Crescer cooperative training program, routes 1 and 2.
