The President and Vice-President of the Board of Directors, jointly, without prejudice to other duties arising from the law and these Bylaws, are responsible, observing the details provided for in Sicredi's internal regulations; I - operationally manage the Cooperative, taking into account its purpose, the guidelines issued by the General Assembly, the Board of Directors and Sicredi's corporate guidelines and strategies; ll - contract obligations, compromise, sign agreements in processes legal proceedings, agreements or collective conventions, assign and pledge or renounce rights, as well as monitor the economic-financial state of the company, in compliance with the provisions of this Statute; Ill - appoint attorneys, establishing for them, in a skillful mandate instrument, duties, responsibilities and form of representation, which may be isolated or jointly, within the limits of this Statute.
