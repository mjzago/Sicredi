Exceptionally, the representation of the Cooperative will be valid upon the signature of the President or Vice-President of the Board of Directors or, even, of 1 (one) attorney, in the following cases: I - before federal and state public bodies and municipal entities, autarchies and mixed-capital companies; II - in the granting of documents for the representation of the Company in court, including the appointment of agents and lawyers; III - in cases of license of less than 120 (one hundred and twenty) days that implies the lack of plurality of representatives (president or vice-president) and vacancy not covered.
