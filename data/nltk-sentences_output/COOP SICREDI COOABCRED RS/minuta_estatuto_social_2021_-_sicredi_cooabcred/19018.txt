The Cooperative will have a Board of Directors, a collegiate deliberation body, composed of individual members, with 14 (fourteen) advisors, including a President and a Vice-President, constituting basic conditions for candidacy and exercise of the position, without prejudice to meeting the additional requirements set out in Sicredi's internal regulations: I - not maintain an employment relationship with any entity that is part of Sicredi, or even with a member of the Cooperative's management or supervisory boards; II - not be a spouse or partner, nor be related up to the 2nd (second) degree, straight or collateral, with members of the Administrative and Fiscal Councils; III - not being, simultaneously, employee or administrator or hold a stake in a company that, due to its activities, is considered to compete with any of the entities that make up Sicredi or in whose capital they have a stake; IV - not have held or are holding a political-party position or function in the last civil year; V - meet the professional qualifications required for the position, in accordance with current legislation and in accordance with systemic regulations, compatible with the complexity of the activities inherent; VI - failure to carry out 2 (two) or more debt renegotiations at the Cooperative or at another Sicredi entity in the last 2 (two) financial years; VII - not respond, nor any company of which it is a controller or administrator, for pending issues relating to protest of titles, legal charges, issuance of bad checks, non-compliance with obligations and other similar occurrences or circumstances, including in any Sicredi entity, or have a negative record in any databases; VIII - not having, through intent or guilt, committed an irregularity that has caused any damage or damage to Sicredi's image in any of Sicredi's member entities; IX - have operated assiduously and regularly with the Cooperative in the last two fiscal years (be an active member) and have participated in at least 2 (two) assemblies in the last 3 (three) fiscal years; X - have participated, in the immediately previous exercise, or participated in the courses and other training and recycling events scheduled by the System, depending on the nature of the position or function, without prejudice to attendance at all other events aimed at counselors during the period of their mandate, within a period of 1 (one) year after taking office ; XI - have availability of time for the full compliance with statutory and iegal incumbers, being prohibited the accumulation with another position or function (elective or neo), which requires dedicated incompatible with the responsibility in the Cooperative; XII - have higher-level training; XIII — to apply for the position of Administrative Councilor, have previously held the position of Fiscal Councilor in the last 6 (six) years; XIV - not negatively expose, or denigrate, directly or indirectly, the image of any entities that are part of Sicredi, its statutory members or employees, by reason of their position, by any means, verbal or written, or any trademarks owned by the entities that are part of Sicredi; XV - not have suffered a sanction for an infraction of a very serious nature, in accordance with Sicredi's Internal Regulations; XVI - not be a habitual provider of services or supplier of products to the Cooperative, whether as a natural person or through a legal entity of which they are the majority shareholder, considering the provision of services or the supply of products that, in last 12 (twelve) months, has a frequency of more than 3 (three) occurrences or, regardless of frequency, lasting more than 2 (two) months; XVII - meet other requirements arising from the relevant legislation; § 1 Ex-employees dismissed for just cause will not be able to apply for social positions, nor will directors who are subject to an internal investigation to investigate violations of legal or systemic norms during the course of their mandate or who, for the same reasons, have been dismissed or resigned from the position to which they were elected.
