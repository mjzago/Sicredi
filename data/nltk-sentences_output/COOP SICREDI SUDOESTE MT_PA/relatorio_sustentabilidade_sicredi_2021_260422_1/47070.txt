Following the succession plan, in which the 2018-2020 executive mandates ended, the president of Central Sicredi South/Southeast Fernando Dall’Agnese was elected in the 2021 assemblies as the new President of the Board of Directors of SicrediPar, Confederação Sicredi, Banco Cooperativo Sicredi, the Board of Sicredi Fundos Garantidores and the Board of Trustees of the Foundation.
