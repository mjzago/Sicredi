Aí Chegou Project With the aim of transforming the needs and opportunities in its region of operation, the Cooperativa Sicredi Pernambucred, from Central Sicredi Norte/Nordeste, and the Instituto Periferia e Cidadania, with the support of Sescoop/PE, created the Aí Chegou Project, which supports young people from the local community by delivering bicycle kits to make deliveries to commercial establishments in the region.
