The grand champion, who won a prize of RS 8 thousand and personalized mentoring from Sebrae, was the “Semeadores do Futuro” team from Colégio Olímpico de Dracena (SP), with the project “Microgreens: How to improve the health of the population through of nutrition", which aimed to encourage healthy eating healthy in a more accessible way with the production of microgreens — ultra-young vegetables that can be planted quickly.
