The processes for managing the socio-environmental risk of the Sicredi system include internal regulations containing rules, methodologies and responsibilities regarding the management of the topic; 0 ISI'Ion monitoring delimitations and fences of exposures subject to socio-environmental risk, in line with the system's risk appetite; 0 Eoleta and use of data to measure, classify and evaluate social, environmental and climate risks in operations; 0 Periodic performance of stress tests for socio-environmental risk scenarios; 0 Interlocution and reporting to environmental bodies, federations, business partners and governance forums; 0 Eli/constant solution of the strategy on the topic, aiming at 0 alignment with market techniques and technologies, as well as stakeholder expectations.
