The processes for managing the IRRBB risk of the Sicredi System include: 0 Procedures designed to measure, monitor and maintain the exposure to IRRBB risk at levels considered acceptable by the Institution; 0 Processes designed to monitor and report adherence to the institution's IRRBB risk appetite in relation to its capital; 0 Definition of the IRRBB risk methodologies to be applied; 0 Systems to perform calculations and measure risks, considering the complexity of the products and the extent of exposure to the IRRBB risk of the System's institutions.
