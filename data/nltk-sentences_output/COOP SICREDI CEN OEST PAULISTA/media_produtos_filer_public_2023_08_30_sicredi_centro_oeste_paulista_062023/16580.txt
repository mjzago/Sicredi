The processes for managing the Sicredi System's liquidity risk include: 0 Definition of processes to identify, evaluate, monitor and control exposure to liquidity risk over different time horizons; 0 The establishment of processes for tracking and reporting compliance with the liquidity risk appetite and at levels considered acceptable by the institution; 0 Definition of fundraising strategies that provide adequate diversification of funding sources and due dates; 0 Definition of an Iquidity contingency plan, regularly updated, which establishes responsibilities and procedures to face Iquidity stress situations; 0 Periodic performance of stress tests with short and long-term scenarios.
