In the event of a vacancy in the position of President and/or Vice-President of the Board of Directors, the advisors among them, they will designate successor(s), and the first assembly that follows will elect new occupant(s) for said position(s), whether or not the designated one(s) is confirmed, and the elected person(s) will fulfill the remaining time of the President's term(s) and/or Succeeded Vice President(s).
