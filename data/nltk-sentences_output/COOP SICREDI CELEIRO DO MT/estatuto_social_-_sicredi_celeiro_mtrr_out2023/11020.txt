§ 5 When any advisor or director falls foul of the provisions of item III of the caput of this article, the Board of Directors, after investigating the infractions, which will be included in a speciﬁc report, will notify the investigated/offender, and may suspend or remove him or her, as per the case, preventively from its functions, while the investigation continues, giving it knowledge of the verifications made, so that within 15 (fifteen) days it can present its reasons for defense, which will be assessed by the Council within the same period or at its next meeting.
