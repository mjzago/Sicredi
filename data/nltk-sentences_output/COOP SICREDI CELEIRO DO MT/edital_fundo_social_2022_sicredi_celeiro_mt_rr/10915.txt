Notice Social Fund 202 2 Sicredi Celeiro MT/RR 1 - INTRODUCTION The Sicredi Social Fund aims to support entities, activities or projects that, due to the amount received, generate benefits, improvements for people in local communities and that bring prosperity to society where Sicredi is present, without necessarily being committed to offering benefits to the supporter (Sicredi), such as the dissemination of the brand and returns on investments, among others.
