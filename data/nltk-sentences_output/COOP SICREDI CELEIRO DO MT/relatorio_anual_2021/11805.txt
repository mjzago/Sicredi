The processes for managing the Sicredi System's liquidity risk include: • Definition of processes to identify, evaluate, monitor and control exposure to liquidity risk over different time horizons; • The establishment of screening and reporting processes for compliance with liquidity risk appetite and at levels considered acceptable by the institution; • Deﬁnition of fundraising strategies that provide adequate diversiﬁcation of sources of resources and due dates; • Definition of a liquidity contingency plan, regularly updated, that establishes responsibilities and procedures to face liquidity stress situations; • Periodic carrying out stress tests with short and long-term scenarios.
