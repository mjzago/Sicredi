To apply and carry out the activities of nucleus coordinator, the applicant must: I - Be certified in the Crescer Program; II - Make use of at least 4 (four) cooperative financial solutions; III - not hold a political position or function -partisan upon election, or during the term of office, subject to the provisions of the cooperative's Bylaws; IV - not respond, due to pending issues related to protest of titles, legal charges, issuance of bad checks, non-compliance with obligations and other similar occurrences or circumstances, in addition to not having a negative record in any databases, external or within the cooperative itself , as well as has promoted or is promoting, as part or attorney, legal action against the cooperative itself or any other entity that is part of Sicredi; V - Not be an employee of the cooperative or have been dismissed from the cooperative for just cause; and VI- Not be a former director or director who is subject to an internal investigation to investigate violations of legal or systemic norms during his term of office or who, for the same reasons, has been removed from office or resigned from the position for which he was elected.
