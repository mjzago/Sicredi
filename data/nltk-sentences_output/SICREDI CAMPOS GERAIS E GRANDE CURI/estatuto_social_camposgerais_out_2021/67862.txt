The Share Capital may be returned to the member of the person who was dismissed, before the general meeting referred to in § 9 of this article takes place, provided that: I – the partial result of the year in which the dismissal took place presents surpluses; II – there are no losses to be compensated with future surpluses; and III – the financial and patrimonial conditions of the Cooperative are considered, respecting the compliance with all asset limits required by current legislation.
