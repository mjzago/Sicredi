The Board of Directors is governed by the following rules: I – meets, monthly in an ordinary manner, and extraordinarily whenever necessary, upon call by its President, the majority of the Board itself, or, even , at the request of the Fiscal Council; II – deliberates, validly, by simple majority of votes, with the majority of its components present, with the President having the tie-breaking vote; III – the deliberations of the Board and other substantial occurrences at the meetings will be recorded in minutes, approved and signed by the members present, the contents of which the President of the Board must also promptly inform the Cooperative's Supervisory Board; § 1° The call for meetings of the Board of Directors will be given, preferably, by in writing, with the use of electronic means of communication being permitted.
