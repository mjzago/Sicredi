§ 5° When any advisor or director incurs the provisions of item III of the caput of this article, the Board of Administration, after investigating the infractions, which will be included in a specific report, will notify the investigated/offender, and may suspend or remove him, as the case may be, preventively from his duties, while the investigation continues, informing him of the checks made, so that within 15 (fifteen) days it can present its reasons for defense, which will be assessed by the Council within the same period or at its next meeting.
