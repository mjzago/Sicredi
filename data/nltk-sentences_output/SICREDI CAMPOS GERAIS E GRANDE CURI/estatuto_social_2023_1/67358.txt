§ 21º The return of items referred to in §§ 1 3, 14, 15, 16 and 17, if approved by the Board of Directors of the Cooperative, will be at the discretion of the collegiate, taking into account, for this purpose, the financial and patrimonial conditions of the Cooperative, respecting as special parameters the level of the Society's reserves and its compliance with all asset limits required by current legislation, with installments of payment by the Cooperative also being permitted.
