XI - Additional information processes for the management of the social and environmental risk of the SICREDI system include: • Internal normatization containing rules, methodologies and responsibilities regarding theme management; • Monitoring delimitations and fences of exposures subject to the socio-environmental risk, in line with the system's risk appetite; • Collection and use of data to measure, classify and evaluate social, environmental and climate risks in operations; • Periodic carrying out stress tests for socio-environmental risk scenarios; • Interlocution and reporting to environmental bodies, federations, business partners and governance forums; • Constant evolution of the strategy on the topic, aiming for alignment with market techniques and technologies, as well as the expectations of interested parties.
