Investimento Raízes - Sicredi Raízes RS/SC/MG, using the powers conferred on it by article 5, § 2 of the Belonging Regulation, invites members from all groups of the Cooperative to participate in the ASSEMBLY OF CORE to be held between March 28, 2022, starting at 7:30 pm, and March 31, 2022, ending voting at 7:30 pm in digital mode, using the Pertencer Tool, to appreciate and deliberate, while Unit, on the following AGENDA I. Exclusively for Unit 001 of the Tunápolis Service Unit, election of an Effective Delegate. Exclusively for Center 002 of the Três Passos Service Unit – Costa e Silva and Center 001 of the São Miguel do Oeste Service Unit – Jardim Oeste, election of a Substitute Delegate. Exclusively for Units 001 and 003 of the Redentor Service Unit and Unit 001 of the Tunápolis Service Unit, election of two Substitute Delegates. This election arises due to a vacancy in the previously elected Delegates, and to cover the representation of the nuclei in the General Assemblies of the Cooperative, according to art. 16 of the Bylaws and in accordance with the Regulation To belong; II. For all centers, define the position (vote) in relation to the matters that will be the subject of the 2022 Ordinary General Assembly of the Cooperative, which will be presented by the Centers Delegate at this Assembly, in accordance with § 4 of art. 16 of the Bylaws: 1. Provision of accounts for the year ended December 31, 2021, comprising: a) management report; b) balance sheet for the two semesters of the corresponding financial year; c) statement of surpluses or losses; d) audit opinion; e) opinion of the Supervisory Board. 2. Disposal of leftovers. 3. Homologation of the Belonging Regulation. 4. Homologation of the Social Fund Regulations. 5. Other matters of interest to the membership (non-deliberative nature).
