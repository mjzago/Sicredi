Sicredi Integração RS/MG, using the powers conferred on it by article 5, § 2 of the Belonging Regulation, calls on members from all the Cooperative's nuclei, to participation in the CORE ASSEMBLY to be held between February 5, 2024, starting at 9 am (nine), and March 8, 2024, closing voting at 5 pm (seventeen), in digital mode, remotely , using the Belonging Tool*, to assess and deliberate, as a Nucleus, on the following AGENDA I. Election of Delegates of the Nuclei and their Substitutes, for the purposes of representing the Nuclei associates at the General Assemblies of the Cooperative, according to art. 16 of the Bylaws and under the terms of the Belonging Regulation; II. Define the position (vote) of the Centers in relation to the matters that will be the subject of the 2024 Ordinary General Assembly of the Cooperative, scheduled for March 22nd, which will be presented by the Delegate of the respective Centers, in accordance with § 4 of art. 16 of the Bylaws: 1. Presentation of accounts for the year ended December 31, 2023, comprising: a) management report; b) balance sheet for the two semesters of the corresponding financial year; c) statement of leftovers; d) audit opinion; e) opinion of the Supervisory Board. 2. Disposal of leftovers. 3. Other matters of interest to the membership (non-deliberative nature). Lajeado – RS, January 24, 202 4. Adilson Carlos Metz President Information classification: Unrestricted use NOTES: 1. * Instructions for participation and voting in the Belong Tool: members must access the tool through the website https://fundacao.sicredi.com.br/assembleias/, registering and identifying themselves with their CPF and password, including the legal representative of the legal entity member (if as the case may be), on the day and time indicated in the preamble, by any device with internet, registering for identification, at which time you will be admitted to the Assembly. Access can also be made, alternatively, through Sicredi applications, through the Assemblies Menu. The digital mechanism used will allow members to identify themselves, as well as exercise their right to demonstrate and vote, through remote action. 2. The Core Assembly will be held in digital format, the modality of which is supported by the Belonging Regulation, in its art. 5th, § 8th. 3. REGISTRATION OF CORE COORDINATOR CANDIDATES: In accordance with art. 14 of the Belonging Regulation, we communicate the opening of the registration period for the election of nucleus coordinator and will close on 01/31/2024. To apply and carry out the activities of nucleus coordinator, the applicant must comply with the requirements set out in art. 11 of the Belonging Regulation, accessible at the link https://www.sicredi.com.br/coop/integracaorsmg/assembleias /, as well as filling out the registration form available at the UA of origin and filing there, within the mentioned deadline, on time between 10 am (ten) and 3 pm (fifteen).
