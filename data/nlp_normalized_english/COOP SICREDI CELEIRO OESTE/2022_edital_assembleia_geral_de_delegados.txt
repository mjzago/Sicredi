In the form of semi-presential assembly, delegates will be able to participate in the event at the Cooperative's headquarters, as well as at any of the agencies listed above, in which the meeting will be transmitted simultaneously via videoconference. At the agencies, Cooperative employees will be on standby to provide support to delegates throughout the assembly. 2. In compliance with the decrees issued by local, state and federal public bodies to combat the Covid-19 virus pandemic, the assembly will be held in a semi-presential manner, with the nucleus represented by its delegate. 3. To have access to assembly, the delegate/representative must present an identification document with photo. 4. At any time, delegates present will have the right to demonstrate, by voice or in writing. Delegates present at the agencies will be able to ask questions through the videoconference system, in the same way, at any time, with the support of the Cooperative's employees. 5. At the end of the presentation of each item on the agenda, and after Once the necessary clarifications have been provided, the delegates present at each of the agencies will be asked to present their vote. 6. Documents and information on the matters listed on the meeting's agenda are available at agencies and on the Cooperative's website, at: https://www.sicredi.com.br/coop/celeiro-co/assemble ias/ 7. In compliance with the rules of public authorities, we advise that all delegates/associates wear a face mask protection, as well as adopting all safety protocols indicated by the responsible bodies to prevent the contagion and transmission of COVID-19. 8. The General Meeting will be fully recorded, in compliance with the provisions of Annex VI to Normative Instruction 81/2020 of the Department of Business Registration and Integration.
