SICREDI CREDUNI - ECONOMY AND MUTUAL CREDIT COOPERATIVE OF SERVANTS OF PUBLIC HIGHER EDUCATION INSTITUTIONS IN THE STATE OF PARAÍBA AND OTHER PUBLIC INSTITUTIONS AND BODIES IN THE STATE OF PARAÍBA LTDA. ADDRESS: RUA ANTENOR NAVARRO, 496, PRATA - CAMPINA GRANDE/PB CNPJ: 03.428.338/0001-37 NIRE: 25400005779 The President of the Board of Directors of COOPERATIVA DE CREDIT – SICREDI CREDUNI, located at Rua Antenor Navarro, 496 – Bairro da Prata, CEP 58400 – 520, Campina Grande/PB, registered with the CNPJ under nº 03.428.338/0001-37, using the powers conferred on it by the Cooperative's Social Statute, summons its 24 (twenty-four) delegates from sectional groups, representing the members who on this date total 8,812 ( eight thousand eight hundred and twelve), to meet at the Ordinary General Assembly, to be held DIGITALLY, on March 30, 2022, complying with the following times and minimum installation quorum, in accordance with Article 24 of the Statute Social: 01) in first call, at 9 am, with a minimum quorum of 2/3 of the total number of delegates; 02) in second call, at 10 am, with a minimum quorum of half the total number of delegates plus one; 03) in third call, at 11 am, with a minimum quorum of 10 (ten) delegates. The Assembly will be held digitally for all members and delegates, using the Pertencer* tool, accessed via the website www.sicredi.com.br/assembleias, with the exception of elections for the ticket of the Board of Directors and for the members of the Supervisory Board which will take place through the Beevoter** tool, to deliberate on the following matters, ORDINARY GENERAL ASSEMBLY Until 6 pm or until all Delegates vote, whichever comes first: 1) Voting for the election of the Board of Directors slate - term 2022/2025; and 2) Voting to elect the members of the Fiscal Council for the 2022 financial year. From 6 pm onwards: 3) Presentation of accounts for the year 2021, comprising the Annual Management Report, the Balance Sheet with Statement of Surplus and Loss Account and Opinions of the Independent Audit and the Fiscal Council; 4) Allocation of net surpluses determined from the 2021 financial year; 5) Proposal for using FATES; 6) Action plan for the 2022 financial year; 7) Setting the fees of the members of the Executive Board, the President and Vice-president of the Board of Directors and the attendance slips of the members of the Boards of Directors Administration and Tax; 8) Proclamation of the slate elected to the Board of Directors and the members elected to the Fiscal Council. Documents relating to the agenda and more detailed information on registration and use of the Assembly participation tool will be available to members and delegates on the website www.sicredi.com.br/creduni and at the Cooperative Headquarters. To participate in the Digital Assembly, members and delegates must follow the following steps: 1. Check if your phone number and email address are updated on Sicredi (APP), Internet Banking or by going to your cooperative; 2. Using the link www.sicredi.com.br/assembleias, register and create a password; 3. On the day and time of the Digital Assembly, members and delegates must access the link www.sicredi.com.br/assembleias again, enter their CPF and registered password, click on their cooperative's event and they will be able to participate in the discussions It is votes on the items on the agenda, observing the provisions of art. 21, §10º, of the Bylaws; Delegates who access the virtual room of the Digital Assembly at the time of its holding, observing the day and time indicated in the preamble, duly registered in the form of the previous paragraph, will be guaranteed the right to express themselves in writing, addressed to the board of directors, via chat , thus safeguarding their full participation under the terms of DREI Normative Instruction No. 81/2020. The votes will be carried out through the electronic systems enabled in the Pertencer and BeeVoter** tools at the time of the Digital Assembly, with written voting via chat being permitted, exceptionally, only in the event of unavailability of electronic tools. Voting for the election of the Board of Directors and members of the Fiscal Council will be carried out using the BeeVoter** tool, through prior and individual registration of delegates in said system, and they must access the voting tool through the link sent to their respective emails or cell phones (SMS) registered with the cooperative, using personal and non-transferable users and passwords. The Assembly will be held in an exclusively digital format, based on art. 43-A, of Law 5,764/71 and IN-DREI nº 81/2020, and recorded electronically for possible analysis by regulatory authorities and/or members, upon formal request.
