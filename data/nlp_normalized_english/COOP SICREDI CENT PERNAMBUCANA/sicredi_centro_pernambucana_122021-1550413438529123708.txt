The year of ZO21 was marked by renewal. Even with the pandemic scenario, which lasted throughout the year, Sicredi maintained the virtuous cycle of cooperativism lives. Through the lines of credit granted to members in cooperatives, we support the maintenance of the economic activity of the communities in the regions where we operate, reinforcing and committing to our mission of adding income and contributing to improving the quality of life of members and society. Our traditional credit lines continued to be active, as did the lines of Public Sector's emergency programs. And, as is characteristic of our business, we closely monitor the reality of the members and the regions where they are present, which made it possible to expand the offer of options suited to the needs of each person in the retention and maintenance of the business. We continue to expand the offer of services through electronic means of service (automatic teller machines, internet banking, application), making many of our demands viable since these are complete channels that allow the majority of operations to be carried out without the need going to the agencies. We also have one hundred telephone channels that can be used to resolve doubts and receive assistance via WhatsApp, which brings even more agility to many requests. In parallel, we continue to operate in person in all agencies, following the guidelines of health authorities and maintaining this relationship channel with our associates. The year of ZO21 was atypical, marked by the partial and unorthodox resumption of the global scene and in our country. The resurgence of inflation has affected all economies, including the most stable ones, trends and IPCA, the country's official inflation indicator, ended 2021 at 10.06%. This result was above the ceiling of the target established by the National Menagerial Census, of 5.25%, which is the highest in six years, affecting the cost of funding and the operational results of financial institutions. The challenges were evident for the administration of the cooperative in terms of mitigating rate mismatches active and passive — with a view to forming a positive financial intermediation result, which, apart from the income from the provision of services and other revenues, would be sufficient to cover expenses and generate surpluses — in addition to the concatenation of pleasures and velvet of resources, however, the cooperative's gross result was reduced from R$6,384 thousand to R$3,188 thousand. During this period we would like to highlight the following administrative facts: we carried out communication actions aimed at encourage the use of digital channels, we offer several credit granting campaigns and work to reduce interest rates, aiming to support all our associates in resuming their activities. During the pandemic, taking into account all health measures, we opened two business offices, in the cities of Belo Jardim and Santa Cruz de Capibaribe. On another front, the cooperative promoted numerous initiatives to contribute to the development of the community where it operates. Next we list the main social businesses carried out in 2021: donation of basic food baskets to legalized institutions in municipalities where there are agencies; participation in the 83rd Enef Week, providing lectures related to financial education for the community; and adherence to the “Natal + So|idario” campaign, through which we deliver donated toys to those associated with needy children
