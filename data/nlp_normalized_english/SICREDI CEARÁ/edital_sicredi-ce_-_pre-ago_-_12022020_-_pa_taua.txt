CALL NOTICE FOR PRE-ORDINARY GENERAL ASSEMBLY (PA Tauá) The President of the Board of Directors of SICREDI CEARA CENTRO NORTE - Credit Cooperative of the Central North Region of Ceará, in the use of the powers conferred on it by Article 26 of the Bylaws and Article 38 of the Rules of Election of Delegates, summons the associates of the Tauá Service Station (PA), able to vote, which on this date totals 481 (four hundred and eighty-one) associates, to meet in PRE-ORDINARY GENERAL ASSEMBLY (PRE-AGM), the be held on March 21, 2020 (Saturday), in the auditorium of ACET – Associação Comercial e Empresarial de Tauá, located on Avenida Cel. Vicente Alexandrino Souza, 28, Tauazinho, CEP 63.660-000, Tauá - CE, in a location other than the headquarters of the Cooperative due to there not being enough physical space to accommodate participants; on first call, at 6:00 am with the presence of 2/3 (two thirds) of the members of each Service Point; in a second call at 7:00 am, with the presence of half plus one of the cooperative members from each Service Station, and, in a third and final call at 8:00 am, with the presence of at least 10 (ten) members of each Service Point, to decide on the following: AGENDA 1. Election of the Members of the Fiscal Council for the 2020/2022 biennium, being: a) Voting time: from the installation of the electoral process at the beginning of the AGM, until 2:00 pm; b) Location: in the auditorium of ACET – Associação Comercial e Empresarial de Tauá, located on Avenida Cel. Vicente Alexandrino Souza, 28, Tauazinho, CEP 63.660 -000, Tauá - CE; c) Calculation of the result: after the end of voting hours, during the course of the AGM; d) Plate inscriptions without fail until 6:00 pm on 03/05/2020, at the headquarters of the Cooperative, located at Avenida Dom Luís no. 300, store 166, Bairro Meireles, in Fortaleza (CE), from Monday to Friday, except holidays and recesses. 2. Other items on the agenda with the presentation starting at 14:00h: 2.1 Accountability of the Board of Directors, accompanied by the Opinion of the Fiscal Council and the Independent Audit, comprising: Management Report, Balance Sheet, and Statement of Surplus or Losses for the year ended 12/31/2019; 2.2 Apportionment and allocation of surpluses from the year ended; 2.3 Setting the value of the fees of the President, Vice-President of the Board of Directors and the Board of Directors; the attendance cards of the members of the Board of Directors, the Supervisory Board and the strategic committees; Observations: a) As determined by Article 35 of the Cooperative's Bylaws, this Pre-General Assembly will be restricted exclusively to the agenda mentioned above, in consideration of the legal and doctrinal principles of binding the convocation act and the unalterability of the convocation notice; b) In the sub-items of item “2” of the agenda subject to voting, all votes will be counted, firstly those “in favor”, then those “against” and finally the “abstentions”, being counted separately for each of the Service Posts; c) The deliberations taken by the majority of those present at each Service Station in the Preliminary -General Assembly, bind their respective delegates to maintain the same decision in the General Assembly of Delegates, as provided in Article 40 of the Delegate Election Regulations in accordance with Article 84 of the Cooperative's Bylaws; d) If the effective Delegate and the alternate are absent at the Service Station Pre-Assembly, both will be prevented from voting at the General Assembly of Delegates, as provided in Article 40, § 4. of the Rules of Procedure for the Election of Delegates.
