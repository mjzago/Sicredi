Desembargador Moreira, 1,300 - Aldeota, Fortaleza - CE, CEP: 60.191 -156, in a location other than the headquarters of the Cooperative in due to there not being enough physical space to accommodate participants; on first call, at 5:30 pm with the presence of 2/3 (two thirds) of the members of that Service Center; on a second call at 6:30 pm, with the presence of half plus one of the members of that Service Station, and, on a third and final call at 7:30 pm, with the presence of at least 10 (ten) members of that Service Station, to deliberate on the following: AGENDA 1. Presentation and deliberation of the names of the members of the Mixed Committee, previously indicated by the Boards of Directors of Sicredi Ceará Centro Norte – Credit Cooperative of the Central North Region of Ceará (incorporator) and the Credit Cooperative of the Crateús Region – Sicredi Crateús (incorporated) , as recorded in the Minutes of its meetings of 07/29/2019 and 07/24/2019, respectively, with the specific function of preparing an economic feasibility study and business plan, relating to the incorporation of the Crateús Region Credit Cooperative – Sicredi Crateús; 2. Presentation and deliberation of the Report of the Joint Committee, comprising the General Report, Asset Survey, General Balance Sheet, Opinion Report of the External and Internal Audits and the Opinions of the respective Supervisory Boards of the cooperatives; 3. Deliberation on the Merger of the Credit Cooperative of the Crateús Region – Sicredi Crateús by Sicredi Ceará Centro Norte - Credit Cooperative of the Central North Region of Ceará; 4. Broad reform of the Bylaws of Sicredi Ceará Centro Norte to adapt the Bylaws of the cooperative (incorporator), provided that item 3 is approved: a) Change the wording of clause “b” of Article 1 which deals with the area of cooperative action, including new municipalities; b) Amend Art. 16, § 1, reducing the payment of the capital of Legal Entities from 50 (fifty shares) to 20 (twenty shares); w) Amend Art. 48 Caput, creating the third Regional Board. Observations: a) As determined by Article 35 of the Cooperative's Bylaws, this Pre-General Assembly will be restricted exclusively to the aforementioned agenda, in compliance with the legal and doctrinal principles of binding the convening act and the inalterability of the notice of convocation; b) In the voting, all votes will be counted, first those “in favor”, then those “against” and finally the “abstentions”, determined separately for each of the Service Points, requiring the votes of 2/3 (two thirds) of the members present at the time of each vote, to make the aforementioned deliberations valid, in accordance with the sole paragraph of Art. 46 of Law 5764/71; c) The deliberations taken by the majority of those present at each Service Station at the Pre-General Assembly, bind their respective delegates to maintain the same decisions at the General Assembly of Delegates, as provided in Article 40 of the Rules for the Election of Delegates in accordance with Article 84 of the Cooperative's Bylaws; d) If the effective Delegate and the alternate are absent at the Service Station Pre-Assembly, both will be prevented from voting at the General Assembly of Delegates, as provided in Article 40, § 4 of the Rules for the Election of Delegates.
