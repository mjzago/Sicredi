CALL NOTICE FOR PRE-ORDINARY JOINT GENERAL ASSEMBLY (PA Santos Dumont – PA Pátio Dom Luis – PA Hospital Regional Unimed – PA Washington Soares – Headquarters Agency) The President of the Board of Directors of SICREDI CEARA CENTRO NORTE - Credit Cooperative of the Central North Region of Ceará, in use of the responsibilities conferred on it by Article 26 of the Bylaws and Article 38 of the Delegate Election Rules, summons the associates of the Service Stations (PA) Santos Dumont, Pátio Dom Luis, Hospital Regional Unimed, Washington Soares and Headquarters Agency able to vote, which on this date total respectively: 1,834 (one thousand eight hundred and thirty-four) from PA Santos Dumont; 1,527 (one thousand five hundred and twenty-seven) from PA Pátio Dom Luis; 1,476 (one thousand four hundred and seventy-six) from PA Hospital Regional Unimed; 162 (one hundred and sixty-two) from PA Washington Soares; and 6,371 (six thousand three hundred and seventy-one) from the Headquarters Agency, totaling 11,370 (eleven thousand three hundred and seventy) members, to meet in the PRE-ORDINARY JOINT GENERAL ASSEMBLY (PRE-AGM), to be held on March 16, 2020 (Monday), in the Carlos Alberto Studart auditorium in the BS Design Corporate Towers Building, located on Av. Desembargador Moreira, 1.300 - Aldeota, CEP: 60.191 -156, Fortaleza – CE, in a different location from the Cooperative's headquarters due to there not being enough physical space to accommodate participants; on first call, at 11:30 am with the presence of 2/3 (two thirds) of the members of each Service Station; on a second call at 12:30 pm, with the presence of half plus one of the members of each Service Station, and, on a third and final call at 1:30 pm, with the presence of, on minimum, 10 (ten) members from each Service Point, to deliberate on the following: AGENDA 1. Election of the Members of the Fiscal Council for the 2020/2022 biennium, being: a) Voting time: from the installation of the electoral process at the beginning of the AGM, until 7:30 pm; b) Location: in the Karine meeting room in the BS Design Corporate Towers Building, located at Av. Desembargador Moreira, 1.300 - Aldeota, CEP: 60.191 -156, Fortaleza - CE; c) Calculation of the result: after the closing of voting hours during the AGM; d) Registration of plates must be completed by 6:00 pm on 03/05/2020, at the headquarters of the Cooperative, located at Avenida Dom Luís no. 300, store 166, Bairro Meireles, in Fortaleza (CE), from Monday to Friday - fair, except holidays and recess. 2. Other items on the agenda with the presentation starting at 7:30 pm: 2.1 Accountability of the Board of Directors, accompanied by the Opinion of the Fiscal Council and the Independent Audit, comprising: Management Report, Balance Sheet, and Statement of Surplus or Losses for the year ended 12/31/2019; 2.2 Apportionment and allocation of surpluses from the year ended; 2.3 Setting the value of the fees of the President, Vice-President of the Board of Directors and the Board of Directors; the attendance cards of the members of the Board of Directors, the Supervisory Board and the strategic committees; Observations: a) As determined by Article 35 of the Company's Bylaws Cooperative, this Pre-General Assembly will be restricted exclusively to the aforementioned agenda, in compliance with the legal and doctrinal principles of binding the convening act and the inalterability of the call notice; b) In the sub-items of item “2” of the agenda subject to voting, all votes will be counted, firstly those “in favor”, then those “against” and finally the “abstentions”, being counted separately for each of the Service Points; c) The decisions taken by the majority of those present at each Service Station at the Pre-General Assembly, bind their respective delegates to maintain the same decision at the General Assembly of Delegates, as provided in Article 40 of the Rules for the Election of Delegates in accordance with with Article 84 of the Cooperative's Bylaws; d) If the effective Delegate and the alternate are absent at the Service Station Pre-Assembly, both will be prevented from voting at the General Assembly of Delegates, as provided in Article 40, § 4. of the Rules of Procedure for the Election of Delegates. Fortaleza, February 12, 2020. Glauco Kleming Florêncio da Cunha Chairman of the Board of Directors
