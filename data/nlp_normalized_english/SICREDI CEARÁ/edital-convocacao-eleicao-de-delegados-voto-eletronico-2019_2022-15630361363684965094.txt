CALL NOTICE FOR ELECTION OF DELEGATES The President of the Board of Directors of SICREDI CEARA CENTRO NORTE - Credit Cooperative of the Central North Region of Ceará, in the use of the powers conferred on him o Art. 45 paragraph “n” of the Bylaws, calls on associates in a position to vote of the Crateús and Tauá Service Stations, which on this date total 1,576 and 458 respectively, for the Election of Delegates based on the provisions of Art. 17 of the Rules for the Election of Delegates of Sicredi Ceará Centro Norte, to be held at December 13, 2019 (Friday), as below: 1. Registration: a) Candidate registrations: from 10/31/19 (Thursday) until 3:30 pm on 11/29/ 2019 (Friday); b) Location: At the Service Station, in Crateús or Tauá, where the candidate has a current account. 2. Election a) Voting: in accordance with § 2 of Article 5 of the Rules for the Election of Delegates; b) Location: At the Service Point, in Crateús or Tauá, where the member has a current account; c) Voting time: from 9 am to 3:30 pm on 12/13/2019; d) Result calculation: from 3:40 pm to 4:40 pm on 12/13/2019. 3. Vacancies: One effective delegate and one alternate will be elected for each group of 200 members of the respective Post.
