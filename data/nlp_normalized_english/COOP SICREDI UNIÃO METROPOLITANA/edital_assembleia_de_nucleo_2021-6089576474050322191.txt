SOUTH RIOGRANDENSE CREDIT, SAVINGS AND INVESTMENT COOPERATIVE - SICREDI UNIÃO METROPOLITANA RS CNPJ no. 92.796.564/0001 -09 CALL NOTICE | CORE ASSEMBLY SEMI-PRESENTIAL MODALITY The President of the Cooperativa de Crédito, Poupança e Investimento Sul Riograndense – SICREDI UNIÃO METROPOLITA NA RS, using the powers conferred on him by article 5, § 2 and the Belonging Regulation, convenes the members of all the centers of the Cooperative, for participation in CORE ASSEMBLY to be held in a blended format *, between February 23, 2021, at 7 pm until February 25, 2021, at 5 (seventeen) pm, through the Pertencer Tool; and the participation of members in the form of in-person voting will also be carried out through the Pertencer Tool, between February 24, 2021 and February 25, 2021, between 9 (nine) hours and 17 (seventeen) hours, in available locations for voting nominated in this notice. THE CORE ASSEMBLY will deliberate on the following AGENDA: I. Define the position (vote) of the Center in relation to the matters that will be the subject of the 2021 Ordinary General Assembly of the Cooperative, which will be presented by the Center Delegate at this Assembly, in the terms of § 4 of art. 16 of the Bylaws: 1. Provision of accounts for the year ended December 31, 2020, comprising: management report; balance of the two semesters of the corresponding year; statement of leftovers; audit opinion; opinion of the Supervisory Board. 2. Disposal of leftovers. 3. Election of the members of the Board of Directors. 4. Setting the value of fees, bonuses, benefits and variable remuneration for the President and attendance cards/honoraries for Vice-President and members of the Board of Directors. 5. Update of the Compliance Policy and Internal Auditing Standard. 6. Other matters of interest to the membership (non-deliberative nature). Porto Alegre/RS, February 12, 2021. Ronaldo Netto Sielichow President of the Board of Directors * NOTES: - The live broadcast will be held on February 23, 2021, at 7 pm, through the Pertencer Tool. The digital mechanism used will allow members who identify themselves, as well as exercise their right to demonstrate and vote, through remote action, in real time.
