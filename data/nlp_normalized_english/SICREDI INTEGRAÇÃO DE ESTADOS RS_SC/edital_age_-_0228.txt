Information classification: Unrestricted use CREDIT, SAVINGS AND INVESTMENT COOPERATIVE INTEGRATION OF STATES OF RIO GRANDE DO SUL, SANTA CATARINA AND MINAS GERAIS – Sicredi Integration of States RS/S C/MG CNPJ no. 87.781.530/0001 -00 NOTICE OF CALL FOR EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL ASSEMBLY DIGITAL MODALITY The President of the Credit, Savings and Investment Cooperative Integration of the States of Rio Grande do Sul, Santa Catarina and Minas Gerais – Sicredi Integração of States RS/SC/MG, using the powers conferred on it by article 14 of the Bylaws, summons the core delegates elected in the respective Assemblies, which on this date total 68 (sixty-eight), to meet in ASSEMBLE EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL IA, to be held on June 12, 2023, at 17 (seventeen) hours, in first call, with the presence of 2/3 (two thirds) of the delegates, in second call, at 18 ( eighteen) hours, with the presence of half of the delegates plus one, and, in the third and final call, at 7 (nineteen) pm, with the presence of at least 10 (ten) delegates. The Assembly will be held digitally, transmitted from the Superintendency Integration of States RS/SC/MG, located at Avenida Brasil Leste, no. 1395, room 3, Bairro Petrópolis, in this city, for all delegates, simultaneously, using the Belonging Tool* available on the website www.sicredi.com.br/assembleia s, to deliberate on the following: AGENDA 1. Approval of the Manual of Technical Recommendations for Career Transition. 2. Granting of proportional funds, bonuses and benefits to the Chairman of the Board of Directors and the Executive Director in the process of career transition. 3. Other matters of interest to the membership (non-deliberative nature). Pass o Fundo/RS, May 30, 2023 . Ari Rosso President 1.
