CREDIT COOPERATIVE – SICREDI RIO GRANDE DO NORTE Rua Tuiuti, nº 765, Petrópolis – Natal/RN – CEP 59.014 -160 CNPJ/MF 70.038.237/0001 -47 NIRE – 24.400.00130 -6 CALL NOTICE EXTRAORDINARY AND ORDINARY GENERAL MEETING RIA The President of the Board of Directors of COOPERATIVA DE CREDIT – SICREDI RIO GRANDE DO NORTE, with headquarters at Rua Tuiuti, nº 765, Petrópolis – Natal/RN - CEP 59.014 -160 registered with the CNPJ under nº 70.038.237/0001 -47, in the use of the attributions that confers upon it the Social Statute of the Cooperative, summons its MEMBERS, who on this date total 15,262 (fifteen thousand two hundred and sixty-two), in full enjoyment of their social rights, to meet in an Extraordinary and Ordinary General Assembly, to be held , on April 25, 2023, at 7 am, with the presence of 2/3 (two thirds) of the members, on first call; at 8 am, with the presence of half plus one of the members, on second call; to 9 am, with the presence of at least 10 (ten) members, on third call. The Assembly will be held in the auditorium of the Barreira Roxa hotel, located on Via Costeira, Av. Senador Dinarte Medeiros Mariz, 4020, Mãe Luíza neighborhood, in this capital, to deliberate on the following matters that make up the agenda: IN ASSEMBLY EXTRAORDINARY GENERAL 1) Adaptation of the Bylaws to the Sicredi Standard Model; 2) Approval and consolidation of the Bylaws. IN ASSEMBLY ORDINARY GENERAL From 11 am to 5 pm 1) Voting to elect the Members of the Fiscal Council for the years 2023 and 2024, with a term of office until the new ones elected at the A.G.O. from 2025; From 5pm to 6pm 2) Verification of the election results; At 6pm 3) Accountability, including assessment of the Activity Report, General Balance Sheet and Surplus Statement, accompanied by the Opinions of the Fiscal Council and the Independent Audit, referring to the 2022 financial year; 4) Distribution and Allocation of Leftovers from the 2022 Financial Year; 5) Presentation and deliberation of the Strategic Planning for the 2023 financial year; 6) Establishment of the pro-labore and benefits of the President and Vice-President of the Board of Directors, as well as the attendance records of the members of the Board of Directors and Supervisory Board; 7) Assessment and deliberation of the Senior Management Position Succession Policy – ​​Resolution No. 4,878/20; 8) Assessment and deliberation of the Electoral Code; 9) Assessment and deliberation of the Belonging Regulation; 10) Assessment and deliberation of the Executive Directors’ remuneration policy (Complementary Law 196/22); 11) Proclamation of those elected to the Fiscal Council; 12) Other matters of interest to the Cooperative without a deliberative nature. Documents relating to the agenda will be available to members on the website www.sicredi.com.br/riograndedonorte and at the cooperative's headquarters. Christmas, March 10, 2023. Damião Monteiro Neto President of the Board of Directors NOTE: The meeting will take place outside the cooperative's headquarters, due to the number of members and the lack of space to accommodate everyone present. If the pandemic scenario remains, the health guidelines for limiting people, distancing, use of masks, etc. will be observed. If it is impossible to hold an in-person meeting, due to any health regulations or any other legal impediment, the meeting will be rescheduled with a new publication of the call notice.
