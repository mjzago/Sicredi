CREDIT COOPERATIVE OF VALE DO ITAJAÍ AND COAST CATARINENSE - SICREDI VALE LITORAL SC CNPJ no. Sicredi Vale Litoral SC, using the powers conferred on it by article 14 of the Bylaws, summons the core delegates elected at the respective Assemblies, which on this date total 36 (thirty-six), to meet at the ORDINARY GENERAL MEETING, to be held on March 23, 2021, at 6:45 am (six hours and forty-five minutes) hours, on first call, with the presence of 2 /3 (two thirds) of the delegates, in the second call, at 07:45 (seven hours and forty-five minutes) hours, with the presence of half of the delegates plus one, and, in the third and final call, at 08:45 (eight hours and forty-five minutes) hours, with the presence of minimum 10 (ten) delegates. The Assembly will be held digitally, transmitted from the Cooperative's headquarters, located at Rua /Av. Terceira Avenida, no. 503, Bairro Meia Praia, in this city, for all delegates, simultaneously, using the Pertencer Tool * accessed via the website www.sicredi.com.br/assembleiadigital, to deliberate on the following: AGENDA OF THE DAY 1. Presentation of accounts for the year ended December 31, 2020, comprising: a) management report; b) balance sheet for the two semesters of the corresponding financial year; c) statement of leftovers; d) audit opinion; e) opinion of the Supervisory Board. 2. Disposal of leftovers. 3. Election of members of the Fiscal Councils. 4. Setting the value of fees, bonuses, benefits and variable remuneration for the President and Vice-President and attendance cards for members of the Board of Directors and Supervisory Board. 5. Policy Update of Compliance and the Internal Audit Standard. 6. Re-ratification of the deliberation made in item “2. Destination of leftovers” from the AGEO of 04/27/2020, referring to the percentages of the allocation criteria for remaining leftovers. 7. Other matters of interest to the membership (non-deliberative nature). This document was digitally signed by Jose Domingos De Andrade.
