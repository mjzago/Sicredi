Credit, Savings and Investment Cooperative of Vale do Rio Pardo – Sicredi Vale do Rio Pardo RS, using the powers conferred on it by article 14 of the Bylaws, summons the delegates of nucleus, which on this date totals 51 (fifty-one), to meet in an EXTRA ORDINARY GENERAL ASSEMBLY, to be held on December 11, 2023, in the auditorium of the Administrative Headquarters of Sicredi Vale do Rio Pardo, located on Av Rubem Guilherme Kaempf, nº 11, Germânia neighborhood, in this city, at 17 (seventeen) hours, in first call, with the presence of 2/3 (two thirds) of the delegates, in second call, at 18 (eighteen) hours, with the presence of half of the delegates most one, and, on a third and final call, at 7 pm (nineteen) hours, with the presence of at least 10 (ten) delegates, to deliberate on the following: AGENDA 1. Reform and consolidation of the Company's Bylaws Cooperative, comprising the following provisions: a) Art. 44, Item I - change in the minimum percentage for allocation of surpluses to the Reserve Fund; b) Art. 44, Item II - change in the minimum percentage of allocation of leftovers to FATES; c) Art. 44 – creation of the Social Fund and renumeration of items. 2. Homologation of the Social Fund Regulations. 3. Other matters of interest to the membership (non-deliberative nature). Santa Cr uz do Sul - RS, November 30, 2023 . Heitor Álvaro Petry President NOTE: The proposed Bylaws are available at the Cooperative's headquarters.
